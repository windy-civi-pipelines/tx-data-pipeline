By: Leo Wilson H.R. No. 9
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Residents of Winnie are gathering at the Texas Rice
  Festival in Winnie-Stowell Park on October 4, 2025, for the
  dedication of an Official Texas Historical Marker from the Texas
  Historical Commission recognizing this venerable annual event; and
         WHEREAS, First cultivated locally around 1900, rice has long
  been essential to the regional economy; in 1968, farmers from
  Chambers, Liberty, and Jefferson Counties joined together to
  celebrate the rice harvest with a festival, and they obtained a
  charter for the event in October 1969; the inaugural Rice Festival
  was held in Winnie on October 3, 1970, and the event has taken place
  nearly every year since, interrupted only by three hurricanes and
  the COVID-19 pandemic; and
         WHEREAS, Over the decades, the festival has become a
  tremendous success, promoting the industry by drawing thousands of
  attendees with attractions that include parades, street dances,
  rice cooking contests, youth pageants, livestock shows, carnivals,
  art displays, and the recognition of area Farmers of the Year; in
  addition, crowds have been entertained by the likes of Jerry Jeff
  Walker, Jerry Lee Lewis, Wayne Toups, Blake Shelton, Lee Greenwood,
  Mickey Gilley, and the U.S. Navy Band, and the event has hosted such
  notable guests as Governors Mark White and William P. Clements Jr.
  and U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Lloyd M. Bentsen; moreover, the
  festival has demonstrated its support of the community by
  contributing more than $1 million in scholarships, donations, and
  other investments to the area; and
         WHEREAS, For 55 years, the Texas Rice Festival has brought
  together residents and visitors alike for a fun-filled weekend of
  food, music, and entertainment, and the placement of a marker will
  allow future attendees to further appreciate the community's rich
  heritage of rice farming; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the dedication
  of an Official Texas Historical Marker for the Texas Rice Festival
  and commend all those involved in securing this designation for
  their efforts; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for the festival organizers as an expression of high
  regard by the Texas House of Representatives.
