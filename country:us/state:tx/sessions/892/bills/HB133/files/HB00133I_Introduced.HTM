By: Harrison H.B. No. 133
 
 
 
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 
AN ACT
  relating to the repeal of the Texas Quantum Initiative.
         BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:
         SECTION 1.  Subchapter HH, Chapter 481, Government Code, as
  effective September 1, 2025, is repealed.
         SECTION 2.  Any money appropriated to the Texas Quantum
  Initiative is redirected to the Texas Education Agency for the
  state fiscal biennium beginning September 1, 2025, to reduce the
  state compression percentage under Section 48.255, Education Code,
  to the lowest possible percentage.
         SECTION 3.  This Act takes effect on the 91st day after the
  last day of the legislative session.
