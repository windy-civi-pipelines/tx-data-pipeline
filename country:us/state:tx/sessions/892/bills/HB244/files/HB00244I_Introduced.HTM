A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 
AN ACT
  relating to flood control measures for Texas Department of
  Transportation highway projects.
         BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:
         SECTION 1.  Section 201.611, Transportation Code, is amended
  to read as follows:
         Sec. 201.611.  [COORDINATION OF] FLOOD CONTROL. (a) In the
  construction of its highway projects, the department shall:
               (1)  coordinate with local flood control authorities to
  minimize the impact of flooding; and
               (2)  add stormwater outfalls and other appropriate
  flood control measures to the project.
         (b)  In adding flood control measures under Subsection
  (a)(2), the department shall take into consideration existing,
  proposed, and expected roadways in reasonable proximity to the
  highway project.
         SECTION 2.  This Act takes effect on the 91st day after the
  last day of the legislative session.
