Title: 89(2) HB 90 - Introduced version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HB 90 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20122 CJC-D By: Martinez Fischer H.B. No. 90 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED AN ACT relating to the postponement of the effective date of certain changes in law regarding the effects of a disaster and related costs on the calculation of certain tax rates and the procedure for the adoption of a tax rate by a taxing unit. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS: SECTION 1.  Section 4, Chapter 209 (H.B. 30), Acts of the 89th Legislature, Regular Session, 2025, is amended to read as follows: Sec. 4.  This Act takes effect January 1, 2028 [2026]. SECTION 2.  This Act takes effect immediately if it receives a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution.  If this Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this Act takes effect on the 91st day after the last day of the legislative session. ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HB 90 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20122 CJC-D By: Martinez Fischer H.B. No. 90 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED AN ACT relating to the postponement of the effective date of certain changes in law regarding the effects of a disaster and related costs on the calculation of certain tax rates and the procedure for the adoption of a tax rate by a taxing unit.
