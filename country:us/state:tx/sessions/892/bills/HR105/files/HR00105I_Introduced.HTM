R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, The Palmview 16U baseball team achieved great
  distinction by winning the 2025 Colt League Baseball World Series,
  held in Marion, Illinois; and
         WHEREAS, A group of talented 16-and-under ballplayers, the
  Palmview athletes began their journey to the title by capturing the
  South Zone tournament championship; in the opening Colt World
  Series contest against Fukuoka, Japan, on August 1, the team
  surrendered just one hit in a commanding 5-1 win; Palmview then
  squared off against Southern Illinois on August 3, and hot hitting
  in the bottom of the fourth inning propelled the squad to an 8-4
  victory; after dropping a game against Youngstown, Ohio, Palmview
  erupted for six runs in the third inning of the rematch to secure an
  11-4 decision and clinch a spot in the championship game; and
         WHEREAS, On August 5, Palmview took the field against
  Guaynabo, Puerto Rico, with the crown on the line; trailing by two
  runs in the bottom of the third inning, the Texas players scored
  three runs to take the lead, and clutch pitching the rest of the way
  led to a 5-2 triumph and the Colt League World Series championship;
  and
         WHEREAS, Excelling with a true team effort, Palmview
  benefited from the valuable contributions made by each member of
  the roster: Mateo Herrera, Isaiah Olivares, Alex Bocanegra, Jayden
  Gutierrez, Samuel De Leon, Axel Pulido, Gael Zambrano, Ramiro
  Alejandro Leon, Osi Lopez, Joaquin Barrientos, Javier Lopez,
  Matthew Azuara, and Edgar Quintanilla; these determined athletes
  were ably guided by coaches Juan Lopez and Brian Valencia; and
         WHEREAS, The members of the Palmview baseball team proudly
  represented their state and the Rio Grande Valley in one of the
  premier showcases for youth athletics, and they will treasure their
  memories of this accomplishment for many years to come; now,
  therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate the Palmview
  16U baseball team on winning the 2025 Colt League Baseball World
  Series and extend to the players and coaches sincere best wishes for
  the future; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for the team as an expression of high regard by the Texas
  House of Representatives.
