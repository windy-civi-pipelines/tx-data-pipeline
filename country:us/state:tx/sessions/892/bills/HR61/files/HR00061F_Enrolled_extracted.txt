Section 1:
89(2) HR 61 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 61 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Sergeant Daniel Gaskamp is retiring from the Washington County Sheriff's Office on August 31, 2025, concluding a half century of distinguished service in law enforcement; and WHEREAS, This dedicated peace officer began his career with the Brenham Police Department, where he worked for 37 years; in 1995, he was recognized with the Counter Attack on Crime Award from Associated Texans Against Crime; following his service with Brenham PD, he joined the Washington County Sheriff's Office, and he has served that agency for more than a decade; and WHEREAS, Sergeant Gaskamp's skill, expertise, and commitment to public safety have greatly benefited the citizens of Brenham and Washington County, and he may indeed reflect with pride on his many accomplishments as he embarks on a well-deserved retirement; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Sergeant Daniel Gaskamp on his retirement from the Washington County Sheriff's Office and extend to him sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Sergeant Gaskamp as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. Wharton ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 61 was adopted by the House on August 27, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House
