Title: 89(2) HR 129 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 129 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 129 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Words cannot adequately express the profound sense of sorrow felt by the family and friends of Abby Lynn Pohl of Austin, who was tragically lost in the July 4, 2025, flooding in Kerr County; and WHEREAS, The daughter of Kristin and Matthew Pohl, Abby Pohl was born on December 20, 2016; she was baptized at the Episcopal Church of the Good Shepherd and drew strength from her faith throughout her life; the youngest member of her family, she shared a deep bond with her older sisters, Hannah and Reese, and she also benefited from the support of her grandparents, aunts, uncles, and cousins; and WHEREAS, Sweet and compassionate and possessing an engaging sense of humor, Abby filled every room with energy and laughter; she made friends easily and frequently hosted and attended sleepover get-togethers; passionate about dance, she took part in recitals as well as informal parties and performances with her sisters and friends, and her love of music was further demonstrated through her singing and the many hours she spent listening to Taylor Swift; and WHEREAS, Abby competed in soccer, basketball, tennis, and golf, and she honed her skills in the latter two sports through weekend practice sessions with her father; she enjoyed spending time outdoors, including during visits to the family ranch and vacations in Crested Butte, Colorado; her other favorite activities included playing with her dolls, reading, watching scary movies, eating Crumbl cookies, and playing Rummikub; and WHEREAS, Although her time on this earth was far too brief, Abby Pohl was a ray of light in the lives of those who held her dear, and they will never forget the joy, laughter, and love that she shared with them; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of Abby Lynn Pohl and extend heartfelt condolences to the members of her family: to her parents, Kristin and Matthew Pohl; to her sisters, Hannah and Reese Pohl; to her grandparents, Leslie and David Pohl and Stephanie and Phil Haag; to her aunts and uncles, Tracy and Todd Engemoen, Julie and Luke Musselman, Luke Pohl, and Memory and Jake Pohl; to her cousins, Lyla and Callie Engemoen, Leslie, Perry, and Mia Musselman, George Pohl, and William and Molly Pohl; and to her other relatives and friends; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for her family and that when the Texas House of Representatives adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Abby Pohl. Howard ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 129 was unanimously adopted by a rising vote of the House on September 3, 2025. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 129 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 129 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Words cannot adequately express the profound sense of sorrow felt by the family and friends of Abby Lynn Pohl of Austin, who was tragically lost in the July 4, 2025, flooding in Kerr County;
