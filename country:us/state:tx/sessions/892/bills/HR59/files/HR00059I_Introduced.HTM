Title: 89(2) HR 59 - Introduced version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 59 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20529 JRI-D By: Lopez of Cameron H.R. No. 59 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, The Raymondville Early College High School Bearkat Band has been named the 2026 3A Honor Band by the Texas Music Educators Association; and WHEREAS, Established in the late 1950s, the TMEA Honor Band program annually recognizes groups and instructors that have exemplified the highest standards of music education and performance; designation as an Honor Band is one of the highest recognitions that a music program in Texas can achieve; and WHEREAS, The Raymondville Bearkat Band was selected as the Honor Band in the 3A class; during the competition, the ensemble achieved the rare distinction of receiving first-place rankings from all five judges in both the semifinal and final rounds; in addition, the band has been invited to perform at the TMEA Convention in San Antonio in February 2026; these talented young Texans benefit from the able leadership of director Benjamin Keltner and assistant director Leonel Barrera; and WHEREAS, Through the tremendous skill and dedication of its members, the Raymondville Bearkat Band has proven itself worthy of this prestigious honor, and it is truly a privilege to commend the group for its outstanding record of achievement; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate the Raymondville Early College High School Bearkat Band on being named the 2026 3A Honor Band by the Texas Music Educators Association and extend to all those involved in the program sincere best wishes for continued success; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for the ensemble as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 59 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20529 JRI-D By: Lopez of Cameron H.R. No. 59 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, The Raymondville Early College High School Bearkat Band has been named the 2026 3A Honor Band by the Texas Music Educators Association;
