H.R. No. 74
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Sadie Schiermeyer was crowned Miss Texas 2025 at the
  competition held at the Charles W. Eisemann Center for Performing
  Arts in Richardson on June 28, 2025; and
         WHEREAS, Ms. Schiermeyer represented Richardson in the
  statewide event, and as the daughter of Miss Texas 1994 Arian
  (Archer) Orlando, she is the first legacy titleholder in the
  program's history; she will represent the Lone Star State at the
  Miss America competition in September; and
         WHEREAS, As part of her participation in the pageant,
  Ms. Schiermeyer established the "Healthier Texas: How Are You,
  Really?" initiative, which aims to expand preventive health
  education that is accessible to children and families; to meet this
  objective, she works to equip individuals with practical tools for
  improving their mental, emotional, and physical well-being by
  conducting speaking engagements and wellness workshops for her
  F.U.E.L. for Life school program; in her role as Miss Texas, she
  continues to further her mission of empowering Texans to live
  healthier lives by traveling across the state to amplify
  conversations around holistic health and to advocate for policy
  changes; and
         WHEREAS, A native of Frisco, Ms. Schiermeyer graduated summa
  cum laude from Belmont University in 2024 with a bachelor's degree
  in psychology with a minor in strength and conditioning; she
  intends to pursue a master's degree in professional counseling from
  Dallas Baptist University following the conclusion of her tenure as
  Miss Texas, with the goal of eventually serving as a licensed
  therapist; and
         WHEREAS, Sadie Schiermeyer has proven herself to be a
  deserving recipient of the Miss Texas crown, and the hard work,
  determination, and perseverance that she has demonstrated in
  attaining this goal are certain to serve her well in her future
  endeavors; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Sadie
  Schiermeyer on her selection as Miss Texas 2025 and extend to her
  sincere best wishes for continued success; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for Ms. Schiermeyer as an expression of high regard by the
  Texas House of Representatives.
 
  Patterson
