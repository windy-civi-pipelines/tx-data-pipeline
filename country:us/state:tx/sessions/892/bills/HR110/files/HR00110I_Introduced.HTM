By: Perez of El Paso H.R. No. 110
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Edith Miranda of Sgt. Roberto Ituarte Elementary
  School in El Paso has been named the 2025 Elementary Teacher of the
  Year in the Socorro Independent School District; and
         WHEREAS, A solid education is vital for young people to
  become successful and productive adults, and those men and women
  who dedicate their careers to teaching make a positive difference
  in the lives of their students and in the greater community; and
         WHEREAS, A bilingual prekindergarten to fourth-grade teacher
  at Sgt. Roberto Ituarte Elementary, Ms. Miranda has served as an
  educator for 12 years; her selection for this prestigious honor has
  been attributed to her enthusiasm and her ability to motivate her
  students to maintain a positive attitude, even when faced with
  challenges; and
         WHEREAS, Edith Miranda exemplifies the passion,
  determination, and innovative spirit that are the hallmarks of our
  best teachers, and she has set a standard of excellence to which all
  in her profession may aspire; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Edith Miranda
  on her selection as the 2025 Elementary Teacher of the Year in the
  Socorro Independent School District and that she be extended
  sincere best wishes for continued success with her important work;
  and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for Ms. Miranda as an expression of high regard by the
  Texas House of Representatives.
