SENATE RESOLUTION
         WHEREAS, The Senate of the State of Texas is pleased to
  recognize Mari Santos, who has been honored as the 2025 Class 5A
  Dean Weese Outstanding Coach; and
         WHEREAS, One of the most prestigious honors in Texas high
  school basketball, this annual award is presented by the Texas
  Association of Basketball Coaches to recognize coaches who go
  above and beyond in their leadership and commitment to student
  athletes; and
         WHEREAS, Mari Santos leads Alice High School's Lady
  Coyotes basketball team with exceptional skill and dedication,
  and under her guidance, the team persevered to reach the
  2024-2025 University Interscholastic League Class 5A playoffs;
  and
         WHEREAS, Admired for her sincere investment in the
  well-being of her students and athletes, Coach Santos is a
  trusted mentor and role model and has imparted wisdom and
  guidance that fosters confidence and success in every facet of
  life for the young women she coaches; and
         WHEREAS, Mari Santos represents the highest ideals of a
  Texas high school coach; her selection as a recipient of a Dean
  Weese Outstanding Coach award and her notable achievements are a
  source of pride to her colleagues, the Coastal Bend community,
  and the Lone Star State, and it is truly fitting that she receive
  recognition on her exemplary contributions to Alice High School
  and Texas athletics; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 1st Called Session, hereby commend Mari Santos on
  her distinguished coaching career and extend to her
  congratulations on being named the 2025 Class 5A Dean Weese
  Outstanding Coach; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution be prepared for
  her as an expression of esteem from the Texas Senate.
