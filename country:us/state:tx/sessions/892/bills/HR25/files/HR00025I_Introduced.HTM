R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Bertha Saucedo, a third-grade English as a second
  language teacher at Jaime Padrón Elementary School, has been named
  a 2025 Teacher of Promise in the Austin Independent School
  District; and
         WHEREAS, Every year, each Austin ISD campus nominates one
  first-year educator to represent their school as a Teacher of
  Promise; this accolade recognizes individuals who are
  student-centered and enthusiastic and who actively participate in
  improving their campus environments; and
         WHEREAS, A solid education is vital for young people to
  become successful and productive adults, and those men and women
  who dedicate their careers to teaching make a positive difference
  in the lives of their students and in the greater community; and
         WHEREAS, Bertha Saucedo exemplifies the passion,
  determination, and innovative spirit that are the hallmarks of our
  best teachers, and she has set a standard of excellence to which all
  in her profession may aspire; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Bertha
  Saucedo on her selection as the 2025 Teacher of Promise at Jaime
  Padrón Elementary School and extend to her sincere best wishes for
  continued success with her important work; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for Ms. Saucedo as an expression of high regard by the
  Texas House of Representatives.
