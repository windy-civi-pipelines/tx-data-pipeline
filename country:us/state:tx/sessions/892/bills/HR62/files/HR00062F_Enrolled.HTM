Title: 89(2) HR 62 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 62 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 62 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, El Mercado in Austin celebrated its 40th anniversary on August 13, 2025; and WHEREAS, Established by the Villegas family in 1985, El Mercado originated as a Mexican market on South First Street, just blocks from their home in a corridor that was largely residential; customers who came in for masa, spices, and other groceries could also pick up food prepared in a small kitchen; and WHEREAS, The popularity of its Tex-Mex dishes led Camilo Villegas and his son, Tony, to clear out some shelves and add seating for diners; they ultimately transitioned to exclusively offering restaurant service and added patio dining; and WHEREAS, In 1990, the restaurant opened another location on Burnet Road in North Austin, and three years later, it expanded to a site on Lavaca Street, near the State Capitol; at one point, the Villegas family operated five locations; and WHEREAS, Camilo Villegas greeted customers and took care of any repairs until he died in 2007, at the age of 82; his wife, Gudelia Villasana Villegas, continued to drive herself to work at the restaurant's office, and she passed away in 2019 at the age of 92; rising real estate values downtown prompted the sale of the El Mercado site on Lavaca in 2022, and the employees there transferred to other locations; and WHEREAS, Today, Tony Villegas remains an able steward of the company's two oldest restaurants; El Mercado South also encompasses a popular live music venue, the Backstage, where long-running residencies include the Monday Night Supper Club, Durawa Tuesdays, and the Purgatory Players gospel brunch, which raises funds for the Central Texas Food Bank; and WHEREAS, While the Austin restaurant scene has changed dramatically over the course of four decades, El Mercado remains one of its stalwarts, to the great delight of its loyal following among fans of both Tex-Mex cuisine and local music; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate El Mercado on its 40th anniversary and extend to Tony Villegas and all those associated with the restaurant sincere best wishes for continued success; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for the restaurant as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. Flores ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 62 was adopted by the House on August 27, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 62 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 62 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, El Mercado in Austin celebrated its 40th anniversary on August 13, 2025;
