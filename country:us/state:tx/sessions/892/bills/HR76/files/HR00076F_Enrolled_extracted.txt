89(2) HR 76 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 76 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Reanna Santos of Angleton was crowned Miss Rodeo Texas on June 20, 2025, at the Miss Rodeo Texas Pageant coronation in Denton; and WHEREAS, For more than five decades, Miss Rodeo Texas has served as a goodwill ambassador for the official sport of the Lone Star State, working closely with professional rodeo associations and community organizations to preserve Western heritage; Ms. Santos qualified for the annual competition after being selected Miss Rodeo North Texas Fair; during the statewide contest, she garnered top honors in the congeniality, test, speech, personality, appearance, and horsemanship categories; and WHEREAS, The daughter of Rudy and Rosalie Santos, Ms. Santos attended Angleton High School and Our Lady Queen of Peace Catholic School; she went on to graduate cum laude from Texas A&M University with a bachelor's degree in agricultural business and a master's degree in rangeland, wildlife, and fisheries management, and she plans to pursue a career as an environmental consultant, helping ranchers achieve sustainability; a former equestrian student-athlete, she has been actively involved in such organizations as the Texas and Southwestern Cattle Raisers Association, the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo, the American Quarter Horse Association, and the 12th Man Foundation Collegiate Council, among others; and WHEREAS, Reanna Santos has proven herself to be a deserving recipient of the Miss Rodeo Texas crown, and the hard work, determination, and perseverance that she has demonstrated in attaining this goal are certain to serve her well in her future endeavors; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Reanna Santos on being named Miss Rodeo Texas 2025 and extend to her sincere best wishes for continued success; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Ms. Santos as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. Vasut ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 76 was adopted by the House on September 2, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House
