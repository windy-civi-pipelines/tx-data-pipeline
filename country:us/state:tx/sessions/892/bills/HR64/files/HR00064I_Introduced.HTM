R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Angela Sanchez has greatly benefited the students
  and faculty of International Leadership of Texas Aggieland High
  School in College Station as a founder of its Parent-Teacher
  Organization; and
         WHEREAS, ILTexas is a free public charter school system with
  26 locations in the Lone Star State; ILTexas provides students with
  the skills, knowledge, and values necessary to become exceptional
  servant leaders in our increasingly interconnected global society;
  and
         WHEREAS, Aggieland High opened in 2021, and that year,
  Mrs. Sanchez helped form the school's PTO; since that time, she has
  distinguished herself through her leadership and commitment to the
  educational community, working with teachers and departments to
  develop fundraisers and assisting with countless events, including
  homecoming dances, winter formals, and proms; she has also
  organized "Spooktacular," a haunted house-themed fall festival
  that draws hundreds of visitors, and a popular March basketball
  tournament in which students compete against faculty members; and
         WHEREAS, In all her endeavors, Mrs. Sanchez benefits from the
  love and support of her husband, Benito, and they are the proud
  parents of three sons, Xzavior, Xzaine, and Xzander, who are all AHS
  alumni; she has been part of various PTO groups for more than a
  decade, and in her professional life, she is a legal assistant at
  the Burns & Reyes-Burns law firm and has been a licensed hairdresser
  for over two decades; and
         WHEREAS, Parent-teacher organizations promote the welfare of
  children in schools and ensure a positive learning environment, and
  it is a pleasure to recognize Angela Sanchez for her extensive
  efforts with the Aggieland High PTO; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby honor Angela Sanchez for
  her work as the cofounder of the Parent-Teacher Organization at
  International Leadership of Texas Aggieland High School and extend
  to her sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for Mrs. Sanchez as an expression of high regard by the
  Texas House of Representatives.
