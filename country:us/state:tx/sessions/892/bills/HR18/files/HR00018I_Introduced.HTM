Title: 89(2) HR 18 - Introduced version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 18 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20297 MLH-D By: McLaughlin H.R. No. 18 R E S O L U T I O N BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, That Rule 5, Section 3, of the permanent rules of the House of Representatives of the 89th Legislature is amended by adding Subsection (d-1) to read as follows: (d-1)  If a member is absent without leave for the purpose of impeding the action of the house for eight or more days during a called session of the legislature, the Committee on House Administration may not disburse funds for the member's operating budget. ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 18 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20297 MLH-D By: McLaughlin H.R. No. 18 R E S O L U T I O N BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, That Rule 5, Section 3, of the permanent rules of the House of Representatives of the 89th Legislature is amended by adding Subsection (d-1) to read as follows: (d-1)  If a member is absent without leave for the purpose of impeding the action of the house for eight or more days during a called session of the legislature, the Committee on House Administration may not disburse funds for the member's operating budget.
