R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Texas Dance Day is taking place on April 8, 2026,
  providing a fitting opportunity to celebrate the important role
  that dance plays in the cultural vitality of the Lone Star State;
  and
         WHEREAS, Texas is home to a vibrant community of dancers,
  choreographers, instructors, dance companies, and students; these
  talented individuals bring the art of dance to studios and stages
  across the state, and through their work, they have captivated and
  inspired audiences near and far; and
         WHEREAS, There are ample opportunities to appreciate this
  powerful form of creative expression, from performances by dance
  companies such as the Houston Ballet, Ballet Austin, Texas Ballet
  Theater, and Corpus Christi Ballet, to shows by local schools and
  youth ensembles; opportunities to participate in dance are also
  widespread, and by pursuing their passion for the artform, people
  of all ages and backgrounds are able to showcase their strength,
  discipline, grace, and focus, while also building confidence; and
         WHEREAS, The art of dance has long served as an important
  means of entertainment and cultural enrichment, and it is a
  pleasure to join in applauding Texas dancers on this special
  occasion; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby recognize April 8, 2026, as
  Texas Dance Day and extend sincere appreciation to all those who
  contribute to the advancement and appreciation of dance throughout
  the Lone Star State.
