Title: 89(2) HR 8 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 8 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 8 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, The annual Fraternal Order of Eagles No. 3719 Charity BBQ Cook-Off is taking place in Crystal Beach on March 20 and 21, 2026; and WHEREAS, Sanctioned by Champions Barbecue Alliance as one of the organization's state championship cook-offs, this much-anticipated event attracts talented chefs from across Texas and beyond; these highly skilled pitmasters put their culinary abilities to the test as they vie to produce the tastiest entries in several different categories, including brisket, pork spareribs, and chicken; and WHEREAS, The contest's grand champion will receive an invitation to the American Royal Barbeque Cook-Off as well as a chance to compete at the Jack Daniel's World Championship Invitational Barbecue competition; and WHEREAS, The Fraternal Order of Eagles No. 3719 is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting local schools, volunteer fire departments, and various charities; and WHEREAS, Texans are noted for their love of barbecue, and the Fraternal Order of Eagles No. 3719 Charity BBQ Cook-Off is sure to offer everyone in attendance the chance to enjoy good food and good times while supporting a worthy cause; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the 2026 Fraternal Order of Eagles No. 3719 Charity BBQ Cook-Off and extend to the organizers, participants, and attendees sincere best wishes for a fun and successful event. Leo Wilson ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 8 was adopted by the House on August 27, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 8 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 8 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, The annual Fraternal Order of Eagles No. 3719 Charity BBQ Cook-Off is taking place in Crystal Beach on March 20 and 21, 2026; and WHEREAS, Sanctioned by Champions Barbecue Alliance as one of the organization's state championship cook-offs, this much-anticipated event attracts talented chefs from across Texas and beyond; these highly skilled pitmasters put their culinary abilities to the test as they vie to produce the tastiest entries in several different categories, including brisket, pork spareribs, and chicken; and WHEREAS, The contest's grand champion will receive an invitation to the American Royal Barbeque Cook-Off as well as a chance to compete at the Jack Daniel's World Championship Invitational Barbecue competition; and WHEREAS, The Fraternal Order of Eagles No. 3719 is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting local schools, volunteer fire departments, and various charities; and WHEREAS, Texans are noted for their love of barbecue, and the Fraternal Order of Eagles No. 3719 Charity BBQ Cook-Off is sure to offer everyone in attendance the chance to enjoy good food and good times while supporting a worthy cause; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the 2026 Fraternal Order of Eagles No. 3719 Charity BBQ Cook-Off and extend to the organizers, participants, and attendees sincere best wishes for a fun and successful event.
