Title: 89(2) HR 65 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 65 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 65 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Leslea Hoffpauir has greatly benefited the students and faculty of International Leadership of Texas Aggieland High School in College Station as a founder and the president of its Parent-Teacher Organization; and WHEREAS, ILTexas is a free public charter school system with 26 locations in the Lone Star State; ILTexas provides students with the skills, knowledge, and values necessary to become exceptional servant leaders in our increasingly interconnected global society; and WHEREAS, Aggieland High opened in 2021, and that year, Mrs. Hoffpauir helped organize the school's PTO; since that time, she has distinguished herself through her leadership and commitment to the educational community; in addition to working with teachers and departments to develop fundraisers, she has assisted with countless events, including homecoming dances, winter formals, proms, and a popular March basketball tournament in which students compete against faculty members; her creative talents were integral to "Spooktacular," a haunted house-themed fall festival that draws hundreds of visitors; for the event, she has designed costumes and constructed activity booths, among other projects; and WHEREAS, In all her endeavors, Mrs. Hoffpauir benefits from the love and support of her husband, Richard, and they are the proud parents of two children, Abigail, who is a senior at AHS, and Maxwell; and WHEREAS, Parent-teacher organizations promote the welfare of children in schools and ensure a positive learning environment, and it is a pleasure to recognize Leslea Hoffpauir for her extensive efforts with the Aggieland High PTO; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby honor Leslea Hoffpauir for her work as the cofounder of the Parent-Teacher Organization at International Leadership of Texas Aggieland High School and extend to her sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Mrs. Hoffpauir as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. Dyson ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 65 was adopted by the House on August 27, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 65 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 65 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Leslea Hoffpauir has greatly benefited the students and faculty of International Leadership of Texas Aggieland High School in College Station as a founder and the president of its Parent-Teacher Organization;
