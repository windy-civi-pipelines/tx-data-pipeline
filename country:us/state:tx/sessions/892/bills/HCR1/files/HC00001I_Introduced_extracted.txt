89(2) HCR 1 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20016 AMB-D By: Bell of Montgomery H.C.R. No. 1 CONCURRENT RESOLUTION WHEREAS, Citizens of the Magnolia area must currently travel to Conroe or Houston for driver's license services; and WHEREAS, The region would greatly benefit from the establishment of a local Driver License Mega Center; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the 89th Legislature of the State of Texas, 2nd Called Session, hereby direct the Texas Department of Public Safety to consider opening a Driver License Mega Center in Magnolia; and, be it further RESOLVED, That the Texas secretary of state forward an official copy of this resolution to the Texas Department of Public Safety.
