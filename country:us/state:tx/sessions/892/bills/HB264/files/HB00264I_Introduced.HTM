A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 
AN ACT
  relating to a prohibition on the receipt of certain state funds by a
  registered lobbyist.
         BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:
         SECTION 1.  Subchapter B, Chapter 305, Government Code, is
  amended by adding Section 305.0305 to read as follows:
         Sec. 305.0305.  PROHIBITED RECEIPT OF STATE FUNDS. (a)  
  Except as provided by Subsection (b), a registrant may not receive
  state funds under a contract with this state or as compensation for
  services provided as an employee of this state.
         (b)  This section does not apply to state funds a registrant
  receives as part of a retirement benefit or other benefit related to
  the registrant's previous service as an employee of or contractor
  for this state.
         SECTION 2.  Section 305.0305, Government Code, as added by
  this Act, applies only to state funds received by a person required
  to register under Chapter 305, Government Code, on or after the
  effective date of this Act. State funds received by a person
  required to register under that chapter before the effective date
  of this Act are governed by the law in effect on the date the funds
  were received, and the former law is continued in effect for that
  purpose.
         SECTION 3.  This Act takes effect on the 91st day after the
  last day of the legislative session.
