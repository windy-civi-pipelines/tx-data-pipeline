Section 1:
89(2) HR 11 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 11 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, The Ball High School Choir is performing in the Rotunda of the State Capitol on December 6, 2025; and WHEREAS, These talented young men and women represent the choral department of Ball High School in Galveston; the award-winning program includes a varsity mixed ensemble, treble and tenor-bass non-varsity ensembles, small ensembles, and solos; students also have the opportunity to participate in UIL competitions, and many of them have advanced to the state level; and WHEREAS, The Ball High School Choir has delighted and inspired countless listeners in the Galveston area through its annual holiday concert, spring pop show, UIL showcase, and various other events; the program has also formed partnerships with local businesses; and WHEREAS, Through their hard work, dedication, and commitment to excellence, the members of the Ball High School Choir have proudly represented their community, and it is indeed a pleasure to welcome these outstanding young Texans to Austin; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby honor the Ball High School Choir on the occasion of its performance at the State Capitol and extend to all those associated with the group sincere best wishes for continued success; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for the Ball High School Choir as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. Leo Wilson ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 11 was adopted by the House on August 27, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House
