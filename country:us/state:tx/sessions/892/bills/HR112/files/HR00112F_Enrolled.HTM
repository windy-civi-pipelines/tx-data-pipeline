H.R. No. 112
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Mary Bejarano of Hornedo Middle School has been
  named the 2025 Secondary Teacher of the Year in the El Paso
  Independent School District; and
         WHEREAS, A solid education is vital for young people to
  become successful and productive adults, and those men and women
  who dedicate their careers to teaching make a positive difference
  in the lives of their students and in the greater community; and
         WHEREAS, An English language arts and reading teacher,
  Ms. Bejarano works to promote student growth by providing an
  engaging and inclusive learning environment and by developing
  strong partnerships within the campus community; widely admired for
  her kindness and positivity, she frequently goes above and beyond
  her duties to support the emotional well-being of her students
  while also demonstrating exceptional teamwork with her colleagues;
  and
         WHEREAS, Mary Bejarano exemplifies the passion,
  determination, and innovative spirit that are the hallmarks of our
  best teachers, and she has set a standard of excellence to which all
  in her profession may aspire; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Mary Bejarano
  on her selection as the 2025 Secondary Teacher of the Year in the El
  Paso Independent School District and extend to her sincere best
  wishes for continued success with her important work; and, be it
  further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for Ms. Bejarano as an expression of high regard by the
  Texas House of Representatives.
 
  Perez of El Paso
 
  ______________________________
  Speaker of the House     
 
         I certify that H.R. No. 112 was adopted by the House on
  September 2, 2025, by a non-record vote.
