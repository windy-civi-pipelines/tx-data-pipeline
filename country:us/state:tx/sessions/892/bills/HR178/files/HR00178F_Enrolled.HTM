Title: 89(2) HR 178 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 178 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 178 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, April 30, 2025, was indeed a special day in the lives of Justin and Amy Farrell of Bryan, for on that day, they became the proud parents of their darling baby girl, Maeve Faye Farrell; and WHEREAS, This charming bundle of joy weighed 6 pounds, 2 ounces, when she made her long-awaited debut at 12:51 p.m. at Baylor Scott and White Medical Center in College Station; and WHEREAS, In addition to bringing great happiness to her parents, Maeve's birth has delighted her grandparents, Roger "Buddy" Farrell and Ira and Gale Herman; in the years ahead, Maeve will benefit from the guidance of her extended family and will come to appreciate the continuing legacy of her late grandmother, Tammy Farrell; and WHEREAS, Maeve begins her life secure in the safe and loving embrace of her family, and with their dedicated support, she may truly look forward to a bright and promising future; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Justin and Amy Farrell on the birth of their beloved daughter and welcome Maeve Faye Farrell as a new citizen of Texas; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for the Farrell family as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. Dyson ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 178 was adopted by the House on September 3, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 178 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 178 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, April 30, 2025, was indeed a special day in the lives of Justin and Amy Farrell of Bryan, for on that day, they became the proud parents of their darling baby girl, Maeve Faye Farrell; and WHEREAS, This charming bundle of joy weighed 6 pounds, 2 ounces, when she made her long-awaited debut at 12:51 p.m. at Baylor Scott and White Medical Center in College Station; and WHEREAS, In addition to bringing great happiness to her parents, Maeve's birth has delighted her grandparents, Roger "Buddy" Farrell and Ira and Gale Herman; in the years ahead, Maeve will benefit from the guidance of her extended family and will come to appreciate the continuing legacy of her late grandmother, Tammy Farrell; and WHEREAS, Maeve begins her life secure in the safe and loving embrace of her family, and with their dedicated support, she may truly look forward to a bright and promising future; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Justin and Amy Farrell on the birth of their beloved daughter and welcome Maeve Faye Farrell as a new citizen of Texas; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for the Farrell family as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives.
