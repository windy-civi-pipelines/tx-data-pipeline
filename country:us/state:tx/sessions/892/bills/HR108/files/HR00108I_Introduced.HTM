Title: 89(2) HR 108 - Introduced version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 108 - Introduced version - Bill Text By: Perez of El Paso H.R. No. 108 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Michael Neff of Montwood High School in El Paso has been named the 2025 Secondary Teacher of the Year in the Socorro Independent School District; and WHEREAS, A solid education is vital for young people to become successful and productive adults, and those men and women who dedicate their careers to teaching make a positive difference in the lives of their students and in the greater community; and WHEREAS, A 10th-grade world history teacher at Montwood High, Mr. Neff has served as an educator and coach for a decade; his selection for this prestigious honor has been attributed to his enthusiasm for teaching and his ability to motivate his students to achieve their full potential; and WHEREAS, Michael Neff exemplifies the passion, determination, and innovative spirit that are the hallmarks of our best teachers, and he has set a standard of excellence to which all in his profession may aspire; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Michael Neff on his selection as the 2025 Secondary Teacher of the Year in the Socorro Independent School District and that he be extended sincere best wishes for continued success with his important work; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Mr. Neff as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 108 - Introduced version - Bill Text By: Perez of El Paso H.R. No. 108 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Michael Neff of Montwood High School in El Paso has been named the 2025 Secondary Teacher of the Year in the Socorro Independent School District;
