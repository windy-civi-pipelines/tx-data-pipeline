  By: Hughes S.R. No. 17
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Family and friends are mourning the loss of Matthew
  Thomas Young of Lindale, who passed away on January 5, 2025, at the
  age of 49; and
         WHEREAS, Born on March 19, 1975, in Paris, to Tom and Debbie
  Young, Matt Young grew up with the companionship of two sisters,
  Jessica and Kathleen; in 1993, he graduated as salutatorian from
  Sulphur Springs High School, where he was a member of the student
  council as well as the football and basketball teams; he went on to
  receive a bachelor's degree in kinesiology from The University of
  Texas at Austin; and
         WHEREAS, After college, Mr. Young embarked on a rewarding
  career in education that spanned 27 years; along the way, he worked
  at high schools in Sulphur Springs, Gainesville, Denton, Cooper,
  and Van, where he taught a variety of math classes and coached
  football, track, basketball, and baseball; he further served his
  profession as a member of the Texas High School Coaches
  Association; and
         WHEREAS, In 2001, Mr. Young married the former Jamie McGraw;
  the couple shared a fulfilling union that spanned more than two
  decades and were the proud parents of five children, Rorie, Khira,
  Tate, Zack, and Kymora; a man of faith, Mr. Young was a devoted
  congregant of First United Methodist Church in Mt. Sylvan, and in
  his leisure time, he enjoyed cooking, reading, playing golf,
  hosting dinner and game nights, and traveling with loved ones; and
         WHEREAS, While Matt Young will be deeply missed, his
  contributions as an educator, a family man, and a valued member of
  his community will long be treasured by all those who had the good
  fortune to know him; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd
  Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the life of Matthew Thomas
  Young and extend sincere sympathy to the members of his family: to
  his wife, Jamie Young; to his children, Rorie, Khira, Tate, Zack,
  and Kymora Young; to his parents, Tom and Debbie Young; to his
  sisters, Jessica Swatsell and her husband, Chad, and Kathleen Wade
  and her husband, A. B.; to his in-laws, Jimmie and Renee McGraw; and
  to his many other relatives and friends; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for his family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns this
  day, it do so in memory of Matt Young.
