89(2) HR 40 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20204 EME-D By: Meyer H.R. No. 40 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Words cannot fully express the heartache felt by the family of twin sisters Hanna Rose Lawrence and Rebecca Mark Lawrence of Dallas, who tragically lost their lives on July 4, 2025, at the age of eight; and WHEREAS, Born on March 17, 2017, Hanna and Rebecca were beloved by their parents, Lacy and John Lawrence, and their older sister, Harper, along with their extended family of grandparents, aunts, uncles, and cousins; and WHEREAS, Though distinct in personality, the pair shared an unbreakable sisterly bond; they had recently completed the second grade at University Park Elementary School, where Hanna stood out among her classmates with her extroverted charm and her affinity for the spotlight; Hanna, who had entered the world just two minutes before her sister, was a go-getter and a leader by nature, and she formed friendships effortlessly wherever she went; she had a deeply caring heart, and she dreamed of one day opening a restaurant to serve people in need; and WHEREAS, Rebecca, the more reserved and soft-spoken of the twins, had a heartwarming smile that mirrored her kind and sweet nature; she adored animals, particularly big cats, and enjoyed expressing herself through fashion; like her sister, she was full of love, creativity, and warmth, and she hoped to some day become a teacher and follow in the footsteps of the wonderful educators in her life; and WHEREAS, Although the passing of these two unique and spirited young girls brings immense sorrow, Hanna and Rebecca Lawrence touched the lives of many people during their time on this earth, and they leave behind a legacy that will never be forgotten; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of Hanna Rose and Rebecca Mark Lawrence and extend heartfelt condolences to the members of their family: to their parents, Lacy and John Lawrence; to their older sister, Harper Lawrence; to their grandparents, Betty and John H. Martin and Roberta and David Lawrence; to their aunts and uncles, Meredith and Andreas Reshoft, David Lawrence III, Jennifer and Walter Prather, Amanda and Bailey Wood, and Dana Lawrence; to their cousins, Sasha, Skyler, Katharine, David, Julia, and Katherine; and to their other relatives and friends; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for their family and that when the Texas House of Representatives adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Hanna and Rebecca Lawrence.
