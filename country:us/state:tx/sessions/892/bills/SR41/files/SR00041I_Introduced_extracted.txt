89(2) SR 41 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20513 AMB-D By: Hagenbuch S.R. No. 41 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Denisse Vargas has distinguished herself in her responsibilities as a legislative intern in the office of State Senator Brent Hagenbuch during the summer of 2025 and the 1st Called Session of the 89th Texas Legislature; and WHEREAS, Since joining the staff, Ms. Vargas has provided vital assistance in handling a wide variety of challenging tasks, including conducting legislative research and financial analysis as well as engaging with stakeholders, and she has demonstrated exceptional adaptability and professionalism; in addition to gaining valuable experience in the field of public service, she has learned more about the legislative process and the issues facing citizens of the Lone Star State; and WHEREAS, Ms. Vargas currently attends The University of Texas at Austin, where she is studying economics; and WHEREAS, Denisse Vargas has performed her duties as a legislative intern with skill and dedication, and she is indeed deserving of special recognition for her fine work; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the Senate of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commend Denisse Vargas for her service as a legislative intern in the office of State Senator Brent Hagenbuch and extend to her sincere best wishes for continued success in all her endeavors; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Ms. Vargas as an expression of high regard by the Texas Senate.
