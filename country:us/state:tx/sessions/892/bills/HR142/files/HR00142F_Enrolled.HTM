H.R. No. 142
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Words cannot adequately express the sorrow felt at
  the passing of eight-year-old Anna Margaret Bellows of Houston, who
  was tragically lost in the July 4, 2025, flooding in Kerr County;
  and
         WHEREAS, The daughter of Patricia and Warren Bellows,
  Margaret Bellows was born on October 27, 2016; she shared a strong
  bond with her sister, Mary, and she also benefited from the support
  of her grandparents, aunt, uncle, and cousins; and
         WHEREAS, Margaret was a student at St. John's School at the
  time of her passing; tenacious and bold, she had a zest for life and
  deeply loved her family members, friends, and pets; she also
  enjoyed playing sports, especially field hockey, soccer, and
  basketball, and her laughter and hugs brightened the days of her
  loved ones; and
         WHEREAS, Although it is immeasurably painful to say goodbye
  to someone so young and full of promise, those mourning the loss of
  Margaret Bellows may find comfort in knowing that their lives have
  been forever enriched by the joyful times they spent in her company;
  now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory
  of Anna Margaret Bellows and extend sincere condolences to the
  members of her family:  to her parents, Patricia Harris Bellows and
  Warren Sylvanus Bellows IV; to her sister, Mary Elizabeth Bellows;
  to her grandparents, Richard and Kathleen Harris and Warren and
  Ruth Bellows; to her aunt and uncle, Elizabeth and Robert Brockman;
  to her cousins, James Maxwell and Julia May Brockman; and to her
  other relatives and many friends; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for her family and that when the Texas House of
  Representatives adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Margaret
  Bellows.
 
  Johnson
