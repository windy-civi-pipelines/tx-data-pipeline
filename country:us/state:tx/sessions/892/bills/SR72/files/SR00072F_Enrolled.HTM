SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 72
 
 
  In Memory
  of
  William N. Young Jr.
 
         WHEREAS, The Senate of the State of Texas joins the
  citizens of Laredo in mourning the loss of William N. Young Jr.,
  who died July 26, 2025, at the age of 73; and
         WHEREAS, Born in Laredo on October 22, 1951, Bill remained
  a lifelong resident of the community he so greatly cherished; he
  attended Laredo United High School, where he excelled as an
  athlete in football, basketball, baseball, and track and field
  and was a valued member of the school band; and
         WHEREAS, Bill enjoyed a variety of career pursuits, which
  he approached with his characteristic energy and dedication; he
  served as an athletic coach at his alma mater for a time and
  worked on weekends as a disc jockey for KGNS radio; he found
  success as a car salesman and as a produce salesman, and he was a
  pioneering hydroponic agriculturalist; Bill found great reward
  in serving his community and country as a United States Customs
  and Immigration agent, a role which he carried out with pride and
  integrity; and
         WHEREAS, Following his retirement, Mr. Young remained
  active in civic life and served as chairman of the Webb County
  Republican Party from 2018 to 2021; he was an avid outdoorsman
  who enjoyed hunting and fishing, and he later returned to his
  agricultural roots and devoted his efforts to cultivating
  moringa plants and tending his land; and
         WHEREAS, Noted for his warmth, kindness, and engaging
  sense of humor, Bill Young was a friend to countless people; he
  cherished the life he shared with his devoted wife, Leslie, their
  daughters, Charlotte, Victoria, and Sapphire, his son-in-law,
  Cole Garlick, and his grandson, Julius, who all enriched his life
  immeasurably; though he will be deeply missed, his memory will be
  forever treasured by his many loved ones and by the community he
  served with unwavering commitment; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the life
  of William N. Young Jr. and extend sincere condolences to his
  bereaved family; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution be prepared for
  his family as an expression of deepest sympathy from the Texas
  Senate and that when the Senate adjourns this day, it do so in
  memory of Bill Young.
 
  Zaffirini
