Title: 89(2) HR 58 - Introduced version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 58 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20354 MAW-D By: Leo Wilson H.R. No. 58 R E S O L U T I O N BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, That the permanent rules of the House of Representatives of the 89th Legislature be amended to read as follows: (1)  Rule 4, Section 4A, is amended to read as follows: Sec. 4A.  ADDITIONAL RESTRICTIONS. If, at the time the speaker announces the membership of standing committees, the members of the house of one political party constitute a majority of the membership of the house, the speaker shall designate a member of that party to serve as chair of each standing committee. [The speaker shall not designate a member of that party to serve as vice-chair of a standing committee.] (2)  Rule 4, Section 6A, is amended by adding Subsection (c) to read as follows: (c)  The vice-chair of a committee is not entitled to receive a separate budget for that position. ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 58 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20354 MAW-D By: Leo Wilson H.R. No. 58 R E S O L U T I O N BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, That the permanent rules of the House of Representatives of the 89th Legislature be amended to read as follows: (1)  Rule 4, Section 4A, is amended to read as follows: Sec. 4A.  ADDITIONAL RESTRICTIONS.
