A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 
AN ACT
  relating to authorizing certain projects to be undertaken in
  response to a disaster by certain economic development
  corporations.
         BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:
         SECTION 1.  Subchapter D, Chapter 505, Local Government
  Code, is amended by adding Section 505.162 to read as follows:
         Sec. 505.162.  PROJECTS RELATED TO RECOVERY FROM DISASTER.  
  (a)  For purposes of this chapter, "project" includes a project
  described by Subsection (b).
         (b)  A Type B corporation may undertake the following
  projects only in direct connection with a disaster that has been
  declared by the governor under Section 418.014, Government Code, or
  by the president of the United States:
               (1)  a grant or loan of money to a business enterprise
  damaged by a disaster, including a grant or loan for the temporary
  relocation of the business or the repair or elevation of the
  business's infrastructure; and
               (2)  action to strengthen the work force in and
  stabilize the economy of the authorizing municipality.
         (c)  In addition to any other requirement of this chapter, a
  project authorized by this section must be:
               (1)  approved by the governing body of the Type B
  corporation's authorizing municipality;
               (2)  undertaken not later than the second anniversary
  of the date of the disaster declaration; and
               (3)  sufficiently documented to allow the governing
  body of the Type B corporation's authorizing municipality to
  provide oversight of the project.
         SECTION 2.  This Act takes effect on the 91st day after the
  last day of the legislative session.
