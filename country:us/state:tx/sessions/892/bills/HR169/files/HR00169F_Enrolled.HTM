Title: 89(2) HR 169 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 169 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 169 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Dr. Ruben Gallegos Elementary School in the Brownsville Independent School District is celebrating its 25th anniversary during the 2025-2026 school year; and WHEREAS, Opened in 2001, the school is named for Dr. Ruben Gallegos, the original principal of Castañeda Elementary in Brownsville, which was founded to serve migrant students; he also held leadership positions with Dallas ISD and Edinburg ISD, and he taught future school administrators during his tenure as a professor at The University of Texas at Brownsville; and WHEREAS, Gallegos Elementary is located on Avenida Rancho Viejo in Brownsville, and today, the campus benefits from the able leadership of its principal, Cynthia Cardenas; since its inception, the school has demonstrated an outstanding commitment to providing students with a quality education and a positive learning environment, and it has promoted student activities such as the University Interscholastic League, chess club, and science fair; and WHEREAS, Over the course of the past two and a half decades, Gallegos Elementary has given countless young Texans a strong foundation on which to build, and it is a pleasure to join in recognizing the school on this milestone occasion; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the 25th anniversary of Dr. Ruben Gallegos Elementary School and extend to all those associated with the campus sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Gallegos Elementary as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. Gámez ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 169 was adopted by the House on September 3, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 169 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 169 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Dr. Ruben Gallegos Elementary School in the Brownsville Independent School District is celebrating its 25th anniversary during the 2025-2026 school year; and WHEREAS, Opened in 2001, the school is named for Dr. Ruben Gallegos, the original principal of Castañeda Elementary in Brownsville, which was founded to serve migrant students; he also held leadership positions with Dallas ISD and Edinburg ISD, and he taught future school administrators during his tenure as a professor at The University of Texas at Brownsville; and WHEREAS, Gallegos Elementary is located on Avenida Rancho Viejo in Brownsville, and today, the campus benefits from the able leadership of its principal, Cynthia Cardenas; since its inception, the school has demonstrated an outstanding commitment to providing students with a quality education and a positive learning environment, and it has promoted student activities such as the University Interscholastic League, chess club, and science fair; and WHEREAS, Over the course of the past two and a half decades, Gallegos Elementary has given countless young Texans a strong foundation on which to build, and it is a pleasure to join in recognizing the school on this milestone occasion; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the 25th anniversary of Dr. Ruben Gallegos Elementary School and extend to all those associated with the campus sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Gallegos Elementary as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives.
