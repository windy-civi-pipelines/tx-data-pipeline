SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 25
 
 
  In Memory
  of
  David Raney
 
         WHEREAS, Memories of a life well lived may help to comfort
  those saddened by the death of David Walker Raney of Texarkana on
  July 28, 2025, at the age of 77; and
         WHEREAS, David Raney was born in Texarkana on November 2,
  1947, to Marjorie and David Richard Raney; he owned his own
  business, David Raney Florist, and over the course of his career,
  he helped design and execute numerous special events for his
  customers; he also served as president of the Arkansas Florist
  Association in 2002; among his many achievements, he was inducted
  into the American Institute of Floral Designers; and
         WHEREAS, Mr. Raney was the proud father of two sons, Colin
  and Richard, and over the years, he had the pleasure of welcoming
  into his family five grandchildren, Reagan, Ella, Jackson,
  Aidan, and Noah; and
         WHEREAS, Those who were privileged to share in this
  treasured man's love and friendship will always remember him with
  great tenderness and affection; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the life
  of David Walker Raney and extend heartfelt sympathy to the
  members of his family: to his children, Colin Raney and his wife,
  Eun Jin, and Richard Raney and his wife, Amanda; to his
  grandchildren, Reagan, Ella, Jackson, Aidan, and Noah Raney; to
  his brother, Charles Raney; and to his other relatives and
  friends; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be
  prepared for his family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns
  this day, it do so in memory of David Raney.
 
  Hughes
