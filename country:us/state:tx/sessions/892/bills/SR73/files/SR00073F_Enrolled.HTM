SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 73
         WHEREAS, The Senate of the State of Texas is pleased to
  recognize John Ross Campbell as an exemplary Texas citizen and on
  his five decades of service with H-E-B Grocery Company in Laredo;
  and
         WHEREAS, A lifelong, beloved resident of Laredo, John
  Campbell embarked on his rewarding career at the H-E-B store on
  Guadalupe Street in 1975 while attending Raymond and Tirza Martin
  High School; and
         WHEREAS, John quickly distinguished himself for his
  diligence and uncompromising work ethic, and over the last 50
  years, he has fostered a welcoming and supportive environment for
  customers and colleagues that exemplifies the grocery chain's
  community-first spirit; and
         WHEREAS, In his spare time, John has made a positive impact
  on his family and his community through his genealogy research;
  his influential work has been recognized by such notable
  institutions as the Webb County Heritage Foundation, the
  Hispanic Organization for Genealogy and Research in Dallas, and
  genealogical societies in Laredo and Zapata County; and
         WHEREAS, John Campbell is an esteemed Texan and a treasured
  member of the H-E-B family, and he is truly deserving of
  recognition for half a century of service with the H-E-B Grocery
  Company and for his noteworthy achievements in advancing
  historical and genealogical research in South Texas; now,
  therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby recognize John Ross
  Campbell on his outstanding career with H-E-B and as a
  genealogist extraordinaire who is committed to preserving the
  state's storied heritage and extend to him best wishes in all his
  endeavors; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That a copy of this Resolution be prepared for
  him as an expression of esteem from the Texas Senate.
 
  Zaffirini
