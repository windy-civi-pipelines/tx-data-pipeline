SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 44
         WHEREAS, Alexandra Bellsnyder has distinguished herself in
  her responsibilities as a legislative intern in the office of
  State Senator Brent Hagenbuch during the 89th Legislative
  Session and 1st Called Session; and
 
         WHEREAS, Since joining the staff as an accounting intern,
  Ally Bellsnyder has provided vital assistance in handling a wide
  variety of challenging tasks, which have included conducting
  legislative and financial research as well as policy analysis,
  and she has demonstrated exceptional adaptability and
  communication skills; in addition to gaining valuable experience
  in the field of public service, she has learned more about the
  legislative process and the issues facing citizens of the Lone
  Star State; and
 
         WHEREAS, Ms. Bellsnyder is currently a student at The
  University of Texas at Austin, where she is studying finance; and
 
         WHEREAS, Ally Bellsnyder has performed her duties as a
  legislative intern with skill and dedication, and she is indeed
  deserving of special recognition for her fine work; now,
  therefore, be it
 
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commend Alexandra
  Bellsnyder for her service as a legislative intern in the
  office of State Senator Brent Hagenbuch and extend to her
  sincere best wishes for continued success in all her endeavors;
  and, be it further
 
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be
  prepared for Ms. Bellsnyder as an expression of high regard from
  the Texas Senate.
 
  Hagenbuch
