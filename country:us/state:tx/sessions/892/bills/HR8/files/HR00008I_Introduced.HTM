By: Leo Wilson H.R. No. 8
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, The annual Fraternal Order of Eagles No. 3719
  Charity BBQ Cook-Off is taking place in Crystal Beach on March 20
  and 21, 2026; and
         WHEREAS, Sanctioned by Champions Barbecue Alliance as one of
  the organization's state championship cook-offs, this
  much-anticipated event attracts talented chefs from across Texas
  and beyond; these highly skilled pitmasters put their culinary
  abilities to the test as they vie to produce the tastiest entries in
  several different categories, including brisket, pork spareribs,
  and chicken; and
         WHEREAS, The contest's grand champion will receive an
  invitation to the American Royal Barbeque Cook-Off as well as a
  chance to compete at the Jack Daniel's World Championship
  Invitational Barbecue competition; and
         WHEREAS, The Fraternal Order of Eagles No. 3719 is a
  nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting local schools,
  volunteer fire departments, and various charities; and
         WHEREAS, Texans are noted for their love of barbecue, and the
  Fraternal Order of Eagles No. 3719 Charity BBQ Cook-Off is sure to
  offer everyone in attendance the chance to enjoy good food and good
  times while supporting a worthy cause; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the 2026
  Fraternal Order of Eagles No. 3719 Charity BBQ Cook-Off and extend
  to the organizers, participants, and attendees sincere best wishes
  for a fun and successful event.
