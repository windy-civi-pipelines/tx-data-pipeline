Title: 89(2) HR 35 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 35 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 35 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Family and friends are mourning the passing of eight-year-old Virginia Wynne Naylor of Richardson, who was tragically lost in the July 4, 2025, flooding in Kerr County; and WHEREAS, Wynne Naylor was the daughter of Cole and Alli Naylor, and she was blessed with the affection of two younger sisters; she was a student at Lake Highlands Elementary School in Dallas; and WHEREAS, Wynne drew great strength from her religious faith, and her life was also profoundly shaped by the time she spent hunting, fishing, and enjoying the outdoors with her family; to honor her memory, her family has established the More Like Wynne Fund, which will provide support to local schools, youth wildlife conservation initiatives, and Christian ministries; and WHEREAS, Although Wynne Naylor's time on this earth was far too brief, memories of her kindness and her adventurous spirit will live on in the hearts of those who knew and loved her; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of Virginia Wynne Naylor and extend heartfelt condolences to her loved ones and to all those who share in the sorrow of her loss; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for her family and that when the Texas House of Representatives adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Wynne Naylor. Meyer ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 35 was unanimously adopted by a rising vote of the House on August 27, 2025. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 35 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 35 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Family and friends are mourning the passing of eight-year-old Virginia Wynne Naylor of Richardson, who was tragically lost in the July 4, 2025, flooding in Kerr County; and WHEREAS, Wynne Naylor was the daughter of Cole and Alli Naylor, and she was blessed with the affection of two younger sisters; she was a student at Lake Highlands Elementary School in Dallas; and WHEREAS, Wynne drew great strength from her religious faith, and her life was also profoundly shaped by the time she spent hunting, fishing, and enjoying the outdoors with her family; to honor her memory, her family has established the More Like Wynne Fund, which will provide support to local schools, youth wildlife conservation initiatives, and Christian ministries; and WHEREAS, Although Wynne Naylor's time on this earth was far too brief, memories of her kindness and her adventurous spirit will live on in the hearts of those who knew and loved her; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of Virginia Wynne Naylor and extend heartfelt condolences to her loved ones and to all those who share in the sorrow of her loss; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for her family and that when the Texas House of Representatives adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Wynne Naylor.
