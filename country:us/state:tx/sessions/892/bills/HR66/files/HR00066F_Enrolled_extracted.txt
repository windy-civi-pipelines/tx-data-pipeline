89(2) HR 66 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 66 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, A 60th wedding anniversary marks a lifetime of mutual devotion and shared experiences, and Ed and Sandra Udell of College Station are celebrating this joyous milestone in their marriage on August 8, 2025; and WHEREAS, Mr. and Mrs. Udell were joined in matrimony in 1965; over the years, they have been blessed with a treasured family that includes their 5 children, 15 grandchildren, and 5 great-grandchildren; and WHEREAS, Mr. Udell has worked in more than 19 industries, and he has served as auxiliary bishop of global missions for the Christ Holy Sanctified Church of America, as well as a community consultant and a chaplain for the Buffalo Soldiers National Museum; in addition to providing vital support to her husband, Mrs. Udell worked in child care for more than three decades before her retirement; respected members of their community, the couple has been active in a number of local church organizations and initiatives; and WHEREAS, Mr. and Mrs. Udell have enjoyed six decades of abiding love and affection for each other, and they are among an exceptional group of men and women who can claim such a lasting commitment; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Ed and Sandra Udell on their 60th wedding anniversary and extend to them sincere best wishes for continued happiness; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for the couple as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. Dyson ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 66 was adopted by the House on August 27, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House
