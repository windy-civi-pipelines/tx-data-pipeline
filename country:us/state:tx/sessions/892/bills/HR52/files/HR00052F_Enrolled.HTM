H.R. No. 52
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, For the 11th consecutive year, The University of
  Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center was named the top cancer care
  facility in the nation in the 2025-2026 U.S. News & World Report
  Best Hospitals list; and
         WHEREAS, MD Anderson has been consistently ranked as one of
  the top two hospitals in the nation for cancer care since the
  publication's Best Hospitals survey debuted in 1990; this year, in
  addition to reclaiming the top honor, MD Anderson earned high
  rankings in otolaryngology, urology, and gastroenterology, and it
  was further recognized for its excellence in a variety of cancer
  treatment procedures; and
         WHEREAS, Headquartered in the Texas Medical Center, MD
  Anderson provides cancer treatment at several locations throughout
  the Greater Houston area; the hospital is home to the world's
  largest cancer clinical trials program, offering patients access to
  potentially life-saving investigational drugs before they are
  publicly available; in 2024, approximately 187,000 people received
  treatment at MD Anderson, and some $1.3 billion was invested in
  research conducted at the institution; and
         WHEREAS, The recent U.S. News ranking adds to a growing list
  of accolades for MD Anderson; the center retained its five-star
  rating in Vizient's 2024 Quality and Accountability scorecard, and
  in June of that year, it was awarded its first-ever Magnet with
  Distinction designation from the American Nurses Credentialing
  Center, which has honored the institution annually since 2001; and
         WHEREAS, The recognition of MD Anderson Cancer Center as the
  leading cancer center in the nation is a testament to the unwavering
  dedication of its physicians, staff, and administrators, who have
  worked tirelessly to meet and exceed the highest standards of care
  for their patients; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate The
  University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center on being named the
  best cancer care facility in the nation by U.S. News & World Report
  and extend to all those associated with the institution sincere
  best wishes for continued success with their important work; and,
  be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for MD Anderson as an expression of high regard by the
  Texas House of Representatives.
 
  Johnson
