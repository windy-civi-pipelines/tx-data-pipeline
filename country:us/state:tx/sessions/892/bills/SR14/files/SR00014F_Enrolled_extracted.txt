89(2) SR 14 - Enrolled version - Bill Text SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 14 In Memory of David Fowler WHEREAS, A rich and purposeful life drew to a close with the passing of David Max Fowler of Sulphur Springs on January 14, 2025, at the age of 88; and WHEREAS, The son of David Taylor Fowler and Mozell Fowler, David Fowler was born in Bailey on April 9, 1936, and he grew up with the companionship of a sister, Terri Jo; following his graduation from high school, he answered his nation's call to duty by serving in the U.S. Army; and WHEREAS, Mr. Fowler distinguished himself over the course of his career as an auctioneer and as co-owner and co-operator of Sulphur Springs Livestock Auctions; widely respected, he was a member of the Livestock Marketing Association and the Northeast Texas Beef Improvement Organization; and WHEREAS, In all his endeavors, Mr. Fowler cherished time spent with his loved ones; he was the devoted father of three children, Nick, Tammy, and Kim, and he eventually welcomed a host of grandchildren and great-grandchildren into his family; he was preceded in death by a granddaughter, Haley Pogue; a man of faith, he was a valued congregant of Grace Family Church; and WHEREAS, Although David Fowler is deeply missed, he has left his family and friends with a wealth of memories that they will forever treasure; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the life of David Max Fowler and extend heartfelt condolences to the members of his family: to his son, Nick Fowler, and his wife, Rachel; to his daughters, Tammy Hudson and her husband, Kirby, and Kim Moore and her husband, Mike; to his grandchildren, Brandon Fowler, Ty Fowler, Bailey Conley, Logan Pogue, Kelsy Williams, Chesley Reed, Kasey Rackley, Kaden Moore, and Carston Moore; to his great-grandchildren; to his sister, Terri Jo Nichols; and to his other relatives and friends; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be prepared for his family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns this day, it do so in memory of David Fowler. Hughes ________________________________ President of the Senate I hereby certify that the above Resolution was adopted by the Senate on September 4, 2025, by a rising vote. ________________________________ Secretary of the Senate ________________________________ Member, Texas Senate
