R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Burnt Bean Co. in Seguin has earned the prestigious
  No. 1 ranking on the Texas Monthly list of the 50 Best BBQ Joints in
  Texas for 2025; and
         WHEREAS, Published every four years, the Texas Monthly
  ratings are considered one of the most definitive assessments of
  the Lone Star State's barbecue establishments; the 2025 list was
  compiled by the publication's barbecue editor, Daniel Vaughn, in
  conjunction with a team of 26 taste testers who visited restaurants
  across Texas; and
         WHEREAS, Ernest Servantes and Dave Kirkland, the founders and
  pitmasters of Burnt Bean Co., began their operation as a pop-up
  restaurant in 2019 and opened the brick-and-mortar establishment
  the following year; it quickly gained attention for its traditional
  meats and sides as well as specialties that include Sunday
  breakfast options; the restaurant has garnered acclaim from The New
  York Times as well as a 2024 Michelin Bib Gourmand Award, and
  Mr. Servantes and Mr. Kirkland have been honored multiple times as
  award semifinalists by the James Beard Foundation; and
         WHEREAS, Burnt Bean Co. has earned a well-deserved
  reputation as one of the state's elite barbecue restaurants,
  becoming a source of great pride to the Seguin community, and all
  those associated with the business may take justifiable pride in
  this most recent accomplishment; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Burnt Bean
  Co. on being chosen the No. 1 restaurant in the Texas Monthly 2025
  list of the 50 Best BBQ Joints in Texas and extend to its owners and
  staff sincere best wishes for continued success; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for Burnt Bean Co. as an expression of high regard by the
  Texas House of Representatives.
