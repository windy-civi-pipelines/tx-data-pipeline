H.R. No. 45
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Members of American Legion Post No. 667 in Universal
  City are gathering together on September 20, 2025, to celebrate the
  post's 50th anniversary; and
         WHEREAS, The American Legion has built a proud tradition of
  serving military personnel, families, and communities throughout
  the country; with more than 1.6 million members, the organization
  is a leading advocate for veterans and active duty service members;
  in addition, it provides vital support for the U.S. armed forces and
  strives to instill the values of responsible citizenship among
  young people; and
         WHEREAS, First chartered in 1975, Post No. 667 has furthered
  the mission of the American Legion through its dedicated work in
  northeastern Bexar County; the group's wide-ranging initiatives
  have made a positive difference in the lives of area veterans while
  focusing public attention on the heritage and achievements of the
  United States military; and
         WHEREAS, The members of Universal City Post No. 667 have
  contributed to the exceptional legacy of the American Legion, and
  they are performing a valuable service through their ongoing
  efforts to benefit their community and all those who have valiantly
  served our nation in the armed forces; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the 50th
  anniversary of American Legion Post No. 667 and extend sincere best
  wishes to its members for continued success with their important
  work; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for the post as an expression of high regard by the Texas
  House of Representatives.
 
  Lujan
