A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 
AN ACT
  relating to the suspension of toll collection while an evacuation
  order is in effect.
         BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:
         SECTION 1.  Subchapter B, Chapter 372, Transportation Code,
  is amended by adding Section 372.0525 to read as follows:
         Sec. 372.0525.  SUSPENSION OF TOLL COLLECTION DURING
  EVACUATION ORDER.  During the period an evacuation order under
  Section 418.108, Government Code, is in effect, a toll project
  entity may not collect a toll for use of a toll project if the
  project is located in the area designated for evacuation by the
  order.
         SECTION 2.  This Act takes effect on the 91st day after the
  last day of the legislative session.
