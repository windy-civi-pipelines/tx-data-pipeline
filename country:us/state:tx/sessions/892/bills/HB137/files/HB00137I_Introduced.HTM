Title: 89(2) HB 137 - Introduced version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HB 137 - Introduced version - Bill Text By: Harrison H.B. No. 137 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED AN ACT relating to repealing the state budget for the state fiscal biennium ending August 31, 2027. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS: SECTION 1.  Chapter 1185 (S.B. 1), Acts of the 89th Legislature, Regular Session, 2025 (the General Appropriations Act), is repealed. SECTION 2.  This Act takes effect immediately if it receives a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution.  If this Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this Act takes effect on the 91st day after the last day of the legislative session. ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HB 137 - Introduced version - Bill Text By: Harrison H.B. No. 137 A BILL TO BE ENTITLED AN ACT relating to repealing the state budget for the state fiscal biennium ending August 31, 2027.
