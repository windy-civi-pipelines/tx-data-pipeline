Title: 89(2) HR 111 - Introduced version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 111 - Introduced version - Bill Text By: Perez of El Paso H.R. No. 111 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Sandra Iglesias of Cielo Vista Elementary School has been named the 2024-2025 Elementary Teacher of the Year in the El Paso Independent School District; and WHEREAS, A solid education is vital for young people to become successful and productive adults, and those men and women who dedicate their careers to teaching make a positive difference in the lives of their students and in the greater community; and WHEREAS, A dual-language prekindergarten teacher, Ms. Iglesias has served as an educator for many years; her teaching emphasizes holistic development and hands-on experience, and she is dedicated to fostering a nurturing and inclusive learning environment for each of her students; and WHEREAS, Sandra Iglesias exemplifies the passion, determination, and innovative spirit that are the hallmarks of our best teachers, and she has set a standard of excellence to which all in her profession may aspire; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Sandra Iglesias on her selection as the 2024-2025 Elementary Teacher of the Year in the El Paso Independent School District and extend to her sincere best wishes for continued success with her important work; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Ms. Iglesias as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 111 - Introduced version - Bill Text By: Perez of El Paso H.R. No. 111 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Sandra Iglesias of Cielo Vista Elementary School has been named the 2024-2025 Elementary Teacher of the Year in the El Paso Independent School District;
