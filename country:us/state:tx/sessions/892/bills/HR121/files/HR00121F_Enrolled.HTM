Title: 89(2) HR 121 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 121 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 121 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Margaret Gaffney Sheedy of Houston, who was tragically lost in the July 4, 2025, flooding in Kerr County, at the age of eight, touched the hearts of innumerable people during her brief time on this earth; and WHEREAS, A native Houstonian, Margaret Sheedy was born on July 29, 2016, to Ellen and Andrew Sheedy, and she was blessed with the affection of two brothers, Charles and William; she shared close bonds with her family members, and her circle of loved ones included her grandparents, as well as many aunts, uncles, and cousins; and WHEREAS, Margaret was looking forward to beginning the third grade at the Kinkaid School, where her teachers admired her for being a helpful and hardworking student; she easily won over her classmates with her kind nature and her gift for making others feel included; and WHEREAS, The Sheedy family home was often filled with the sounds of Margaret dancing to music in her bedroom; she delighted in jumping on the trampoline, swinging, solving puzzles, and building Lego creations with her brothers, and she always made time for family movie nights, competitive games of Uno, and heartfelt discussions at the dinner table; she was also adored by her extended family, many of whom were present to witness her first Holy Communion at St. Michael the Archangel Catholic Church; and WHEREAS, Although the tragic passing of Margaret Sheedy has brought immeasurable sorrow, the memory of this precious young girl will continue to live on in the hearts of all those who loved and cherished her; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of Margaret Gaffney Sheedy and extend profound condolences to the members of her family: to her parents, Ellen and Andrew Sheedy; to her brothers, Charlie and William Sheedy; to her grandparents, Ellen and Charles Sheedy and Kay and Dan English; to her aunts and uncles, Elizabeth and Matt Johnson, Elizabeth and Patrick Sheedy, Colleen Sheedy, Laura and Michael Pipkin, Dianne and Dan English, and Tara and Spencer English; to her first cousins, Ryan, Cate, and Clare Johnson, Charlotte and Caroline Sheedy, Michael, Annie, and Lizzy Pipkin, Dan and Sarah English, and Mac, Thomas, and Elliott English; and to her other relatives and friends; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for her family and that when the Texas House of Representatives adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Margaret Gaffney Sheedy. DeAyala ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 121 was unanimously adopted by a rising vote of the House on September 2, 2025. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 121 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 121 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Margaret Gaffney Sheedy of Houston, who was tragically lost in the July 4, 2025, flooding in Kerr County, at the age of eight, touched the hearts of innumerable people during her brief time on this earth;
