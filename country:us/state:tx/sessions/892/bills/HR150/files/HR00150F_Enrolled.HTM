H.R. No. 150
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Austin Regional Clinic is celebrating its 45th
  anniversary in 2025; and
         WHEREAS, Established in 1980 by family practitioner Norman H.
  Chenven, M.D., Austin Regional Clinic offers comprehensive health
  care in Austin and nearby communities; through the years, it has
  expanded to 35 locations in 15 cities, becoming one of the largest
  multispecialty group practices in Central Texas; and
         WHEREAS, Today, Austin Regional Clinic employs more than 450
  physicians and clinicians who provide care in family medicine,
  internal medicine, and pediatrics, as well as in such specialty
  areas as general surgery, orthopedics, and cardiology; as ARC
  continues to grow, it is slated to open new locations by 2026 to
  offer improved access to its services; and
         WHEREAS, Over the past four and a half decades, Austin
  Regional Clinic has provided compassionate professional care to
  countless residents of Central Texas, and it is indeed deserving of
  recognition on the occasion of this significant milestone in its
  history; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the 45th
  anniversary of Austin Regional Clinic and extend to its management
  and staff sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for Austin Regional Clinic as an expression of high regard
  by the Texas House of Representatives.
