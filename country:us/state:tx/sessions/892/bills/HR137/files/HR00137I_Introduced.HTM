R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, The Summer Under the Stars Block Party & BBQ
  Cook-Off took place on August 8 and 9, 2025, at Poteet Canyon Park;
  and
         WHEREAS, Sanctioned by Champions Barbecue Alliance as one of
  the organization's state championship cook-offs, this
  much-anticipated event featured highly skilled teams putting their
  culinary talents to the test as they vied to produce the tastiest
  entries in several different categories; the occasion combined
  friendly competition with good eating, and prizes were awarded to
  the top finishers; and
         WHEREAS, Texans are noted for their love of barbecue, and the
  Summer Under the Stars Block Party & BBQ Cook-Off offered everyone
  in attendance the opportunity to sample exceptional cuisine while
  enjoying a fun, family-oriented atmosphere; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the Summer
  Under the Stars Block Party & BBQ Cook-Off and extend to the
  organizers, contestants, and attendees sincere best wishes for the
  future.
