A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 
AN ACT
  relating to making an appropriation to Galveston County for certain
  legal expenses incurred by the county.
         BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:
         SECTION 1.  The amount of $5,756,567.46 is appropriated from
  the general revenue fund to Galveston County for the two-year
  period beginning on the effective date of this Act to reimburse the
  county for legal expenses incurred in litigating the case of
  Petteway v. Galveston County, 111 F.4th 596 (5th Cir. 2024).
         SECTION 2.  This Act takes effect on the 91st day after the
  last day of the legislative session.
