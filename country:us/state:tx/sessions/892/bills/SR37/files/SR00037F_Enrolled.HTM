SENATE RESOLUTION NO. 37
         WHEREAS, Avery Kurzontkowski has distinguished herself in
  her responsibilities as a legislative intern in the office of
  State Senator Brent Hagenbuch in 2025; and
 
         WHEREAS, Since joining the staff, Ms. Kurzontkowski has
  provided vital assistance in handling a wide variety of
  challenging tasks, including legislative research, policy
  analysis, and stakeholder engagement; in addition to gaining
  valuable experience in the field of public service, she has
  learned more about the legislative process and the issues facing
  citizens of the Lone Star State; and
 
         WHEREAS, A third-year student at The University of Texas at
  Austin, Ms. Kurzontkowski is pursuing a bachelor's degree in
  government in the Plan II Honors Program; she participates in the
  Air Force ROTC program and has completed several public service
  internships; and
 
         WHEREAS, Avery Kurzontkowski has performed her duties as a
  legislative intern with skill and dedication, and she is indeed
  deserving of special recognition for her fine work; now,
  therefore, be it
 
         RESOLVED, That the Senate of the State of Texas, 89th
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commend Avery
  Kurzontkowski for her service as a legislative intern in the
  office of State Senator Brent Hagenbuch and extend to her sincere
  best wishes for continued success in all her endeavors; and, be
  it further
 
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this Resolution be
  prepared for Ms. Kurzontkowski as an expression of high regard
  from the Texas Senate.
 
  Hagenbuch
