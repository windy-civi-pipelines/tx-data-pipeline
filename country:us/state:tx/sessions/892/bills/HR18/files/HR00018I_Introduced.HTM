R E S O L U T I O N
         BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the 89th
  Texas Legislature, That Rule 5, Section 3, of the permanent rules of
  the House of Representatives of the 89th Legislature is amended by
  adding Subsection (d-1) to read as follows:
         (d-1)  If a member is absent without leave for the purpose of
  impeding the action of the house for eight or more days during a
  called session of the legislature, the Committee on House
  Administration may not disburse funds for the member's operating
  budget.
