Title: 89(2) HR 144 - Introduced version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 144 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S21275 AMB-D By: Perez of El Paso H.R. No. 144 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Manuel Delgadillo of Del Valle High School has been named the 2025-2026 Secondary Teacher of the Year in the Ysleta Independent School District; and WHEREAS, A solid education is vital for young people to become successful and productive adults, and those men and women who dedicate their careers to teaching make a positive difference in the lives of their students and in the greater community; and WHEREAS, A social studies teacher at Del Valle High, Mr. Delgadillo has enjoyed a rewarding career in education that has spanned seven years, and he works to promote student success by providing a safe and supportive learning environment; in his leisure time, he enjoys reading, writing, attending open mic events, and coaching for the PBS quiz competition High Q; and WHEREAS, Manuel Delgadillo exemplifies the passion, determination, and innovative spirit that are the hallmarks of our best teachers, and he has set a standard of excellence to which all in his profession may aspire; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Manuel Delgadillo on his selection as the 2025-2026 Secondary Teacher of the Year in the Ysleta Independent School District and extend to him sincere best wishes for continued success with his important work; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Mr. Delgadillo as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 144 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S21275 AMB-D By: Perez of El Paso H.R. No. 144 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Manuel Delgadillo of Del Valle High School has been named the 2025-2026 Secondary Teacher of the Year in the Ysleta Independent School District;
