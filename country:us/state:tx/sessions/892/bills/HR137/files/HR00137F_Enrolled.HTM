Title: 89(2) HR 137 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 137 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 137 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, The Summer Under the Stars Block Party & BBQ Cook-Off took place on August 8 and 9, 2025, at Poteet Canyon Park; and WHEREAS, Sanctioned by Champions Barbecue Alliance as one of the organization's state championship cook-offs, this much-anticipated event featured highly skilled teams putting their culinary talents to the test as they vied to produce the tastiest entries in several different categories; the occasion combined friendly competition with good eating, and prizes were awarded to the top finishers; and WHEREAS, Texans are noted for their love of barbecue, and the Summer Under the Stars Block Party & BBQ Cook-Off offered everyone in attendance the opportunity to sample exceptional cuisine while enjoying a fun, family-oriented atmosphere; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the Summer Under the Stars Block Party & BBQ Cook-Off and extend to the organizers, contestants, and attendees sincere best wishes for the future. Guillen ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 137 was adopted by the House on September 3, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 137 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 137 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, The Summer Under the Stars Block Party & BBQ Cook-Off took place on August 8 and 9, 2025, at Poteet Canyon Park; and WHEREAS, Sanctioned by Champions Barbecue Alliance as one of the organization's state championship cook-offs, this much-anticipated event featured highly skilled teams putting their culinary talents to the test as they vied to produce the tastiest entries in several different categories; the occasion combined friendly competition with good eating, and prizes were awarded to the top finishers; and WHEREAS, Texans are noted for their love of barbecue, and the Summer Under the Stars Block Party & BBQ Cook-Off offered everyone in attendance the opportunity to sample exceptional cuisine while enjoying a fun, family-oriented atmosphere; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the Summer Under the Stars Block Party & BBQ Cook-Off and extend to the organizers, contestants, and attendees sincere best wishes for the future.
