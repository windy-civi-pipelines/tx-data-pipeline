Title: 89(2) HR 56 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 56 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 56 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Antioch Georgetown is hosting a ground-breaking ceremony for its new church building on August 24, 2025; and WHEREAS, Organized in 2017, Antioch Georgetown held its first services at Carver Elementary School; three years later, the congregation moved to Brookwood in Georgetown's Amazing Grace Building, where attendance rapidly grew, staffing increased, and the church began offering additional programs and services; and WHEREAS, Today, Antioch Georgetown furthers its impact through partnerships with such organizations as Mission 4031, Helping Hands of Georgetown, Soul Survivor Outdoor, and Africa New Life; in addition, the members of the congregation have established a variety of small groups that foster fellowship and spiritual enrichment among attendees; the church is served by lead pastor Andy Comer, as well as a host of other leaders and clergy, including Stephen Castleberry, experience pastor, and Darrell Hudson, care pastor; and WHEREAS, Since its founding, Antioch Georgetown has been a source of inspiration and guidance to its members, and the construction of a sanctuary will allow the church to serve the needs of even more congregants in the years to come; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Antioch Georgetown on the ground-breaking of its new church building and extend to all those involved in bringing this project to fruition sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for the church as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. Harris Davila ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 56 was adopted by the House on August 27, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 56 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 56 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Antioch Georgetown is hosting a ground-breaking ceremony for its new church building on August 24, 2025; and WHEREAS, Organized in 2017, Antioch Georgetown held its first services at Carver Elementary School; three years later, the congregation moved to Brookwood in Georgetown's Amazing Grace Building, where attendance rapidly grew, staffing increased, and the church began offering additional programs and services; and WHEREAS, Today, Antioch Georgetown furthers its impact through partnerships with such organizations as Mission 4031, Helping Hands of Georgetown, Soul Survivor Outdoor, and Africa New Life; in addition, the members of the congregation have established a variety of small groups that foster fellowship and spiritual enrichment among attendees; the church is served by lead pastor Andy Comer, as well as a host of other leaders and clergy, including Stephen Castleberry, experience pastor, and Darrell Hudson, care pastor; and WHEREAS, Since its founding, Antioch Georgetown has been a source of inspiration and guidance to its members, and the construction of a sanctuary will allow the church to serve the needs of even more congregants in the years to come; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Antioch Georgetown on the ground-breaking of its new church building and extend to all those involved in bringing this project to fruition sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for the church as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives.
