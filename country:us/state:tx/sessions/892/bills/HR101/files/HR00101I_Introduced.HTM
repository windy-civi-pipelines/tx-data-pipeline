R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Linda Huebner of the Bartlett Independent School
  District has been named the 2025 Region V Teacher of the Year and
  the 2025 State Teacher of the Year by the Family and Consumer
  Sciences Teachers Association of Texas; and
         WHEREAS, Each year, the FCSTAT awards pay tribute to
  exceptional family and consumer sciences educators; recognized for
  her achievements in teaching, curriculum development, and student
  engagement, Mrs. Huebner garnered the regional accolade and went on
  to earn the State Teacher of the Year honor, the highest teaching
  award bestowed by the association; and
         WHEREAS, Mrs. Huebner teaches a variety of Bartlett ISD FCS
  courses, including professional communications, family and
  community services, and interpersonal studies, as well as
  college-level child development and guidance studies through
  Temple College; in addition, she oversees the nationally recognized
  Bartlett ISD Family, Career, and Community Leaders of America
  chapter and the three-time state champion LifeSmarts team; and
         WHEREAS, Linda Huebner exemplifies the passion,
  determination, and innovative spirit that are the hallmarks of our
  best teachers, and she has set a standard of excellence to which all
  in her profession may aspire; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Linda Huebner
  on her selection as the 2025 Region V Teacher of the Year and the
  2025 State Teacher of the Year by the Family and Consumer Sciences
  Teachers Association of Texas and extend to her sincere best wishes
  for continued success with her important work; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for Mrs. Huebner as an expression of high regard by the
  Texas House of Representatives.
