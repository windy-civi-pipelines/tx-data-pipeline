By: Leo Wilson H.R. No. 13
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, The first annual Galveston Pickleball Championship
  took place on July 26, 2025; and
         WHEREAS, Organized by Trevor Gwynne, the tournament was held
  at the McGuire-Dent Recreation Center, where 34 teams competed for
  the championship title in single-elimination doubles rounds; the
  winning squad received a trophy and new pickleball paddles while
  earning considerable bragging rights; and
         WHEREAS, In recent years, pickleball has grown into an
  increasingly popular activity for individuals of all ages across
  the Lone Star State, and the Galveston Pickleball Championship is
  poised to become a beloved community tradition; now, therefore, be
  it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the first
  annual Galveston Pickleball Championship and extend sincere
  appreciation to all those who helped bring the event to fruition.
