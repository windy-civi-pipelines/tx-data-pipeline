89(2) SR 68 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20864 BK-D By: Hughes S.R. No. 68 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Family and friends will forever treasure their memories of Bonnalee Pell Asbury of Longview, who passed away on August 8, 2025, at the age of 97; and WHEREAS, Born on December 23, 1927, in Rockford, Illinois, to William and Marjorie Pell, the former Bonnalee Pell grew up on a farm with the companionship of her older brother, William; after graduating from East Rockford High School in 1945, she earned an associate's degree from Blackburn College in Carlinville in May 1947; while there, she met the love of her life, James William Asbury, with whom she exchanged wedding vows on May 29, 1948; and WHEREAS, The newlyweds moved to Champagne, Illinois, where Mrs. Asbury worked for Sullivan Chevrolet while her husband pursued an engineering degree at the University of Illinois; in 1950, the couple made their home in Hastings, Nebraska, and they went on to raise four children in that community, William, Katherine, James, and Thomas; Mr. Asbury's career subsequently took them to Longview in 1983; she and her husband shared an extraordinary 72 years of marriage until his passing in 2020; and WHEREAS, Through the years, Mrs. Asbury's cherished family grew to include seven grandchildren, Brett, Tom, Jon, Ben, Kristen, Evan, and Elizabeth, along with several great-grandchildren and step-great-grandchildren; a woman of strong faith, she was an active member of the First United Methodist Churches in both Hastings and Longview, and she enjoyed taking part in Bible study and prayer groups; she also found great fulfillment in volunteering, and her favorite pastimes included traveling and playing golf with her husband; and WHEREAS, Bonnalee Asbury led a caring and purposeful life, and her loss is keenly felt by all who were touched by her affection and friendship; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the Senate of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of Bonnalee Pell Asbury and extend sincere sympathy to the members of her family: to her children, William Asbury and his wife, Luanne, Katherine Schnake and her husband, Marc, and James Asbury and his wife, Cathy; to her grandchildren, Brett Asbury and his wife, Jen, Tom Asbury and his wife, Keely, Jon Schnake, Ben Schnake and his wife, Jess, Kristen Madsen and her husband, Craig, Evan Asbury and his wife, Rachel, and Elizabeth Briggs and her husband, Thomas; to her great-grandchildren, Will, Luca, Hudson, and Casey Asbury and Genevieve and Annabelle Briggs; to her step-great-grandchildren, Damon and Tucker Atkinson and Christian and Bowen Madsen; to her brother, William Pell; and to her other relatives and friends; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for her family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Bonnalee Asbury.
