89(2) SR 15 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20452 KRT-D By: Hughes S.R. No. 15 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Family and friends were deeply saddened by the passing of Christi Michelle Mayeux of Sulphur Springs on June 30, 2025, at the age of 51; and WHEREAS, The daughter of Don Carson and Carol Elaine Overstreet, the former Christi Overstreet was born in Winnsboro on July 24, 1973, and she grew up with the companionship of two sisters, Tracie and Vicki; and WHEREAS, She married the love of her life, William Mayeux, on November 17, 2007, and the couple shared a fulfilling union that spanned 17 years; she was blessed with a daughter, Mariah, and she was a caring stepmother to her husband's sons, Wesley and Collin; over the years, she had the pleasure of welcoming into her family three grandchildren, Layton, Landry, and Lawsyn; and WHEREAS, Mrs. Mayeux enjoyed a rewarding career as a radiologist, and she worked as a certified pharmacist for more than two decades; a woman of deep faith, she was a member of New Beginnings Fellowship Baptist Church in Sulphur Springs; and WHEREAS, Although Christi Mayeux will be deeply missed, those who held her dear will forever remember the warmth and kindness she brought into their lives; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the Senate of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of Christi Michelle Mayeux and extend heartfelt sympathy to the members of her family: to her husband, William Mayeux; to her daughter, Mariah Indermuehle; to her stepsons, Wesley Neal Mayeux and William "Collin" Mayeux; to her grandchildren, Layton Bateman, Landry Mayeux, and Lawsyn Mayeux; to her parents, Don and Carol Overstreet; to her sisters, Tracie Sickles and her husband, Chuck, and Vicki Bassham and her husband, Tony; and to her other relatives and many friends; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for her family and that when the Texas Senate adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Christi Mayeux.
