89(2) HR 104 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S21181 KSM-D By: Buckley H.R. No. 104 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, John Kliewer and his family have distinguished themselves through their 34 years of service as the owners of Patriot Buick GMC in Killeen; and WHEREAS, Under the able leadership of the Kliewer family, Patriot Buick GMC opened its doors in 1991, and over the years, it has served countless customers in Fort Hood and the greater Killeen area; in addition, the car dealership has supported local military families, and it has sponsored a number of youth programs and civic initiatives; and WHEREAS, Mr. Kliewer, the former president of Patriot Buick GMC, oversaw the sale of the business to Ancira Auto Group on July 1, 2025; the sale will afford the Kliewer family more time to focus on their other business, BKCW Insurance in Killeen; and WHEREAS, Patriot Buick GMC has contributed to the vitality and economic well-being of its community for nearly three and a half decades, and the members of the Kliewer family may indeed take pride in their achievements as local business leaders; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commend John Kliewer and his family for their service as the owners of Patriot Buick GMC in Killeen and extend to them sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for the Kliewer family as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives.
