H.R. No. 144
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Manuel Delgadillo of Del Valle High School has been
  named the 2025-2026 Secondary Teacher of the Year in the Ysleta
  Independent School District; and
         WHEREAS, A solid education is vital for young people to
  become successful and productive adults, and those men and women
  who dedicate their careers to teaching make a positive difference
  in the lives of their students and in the greater community; and
         WHEREAS, A social studies teacher at Del Valle High,
  Mr. Delgadillo has enjoyed a rewarding career in education that has
  spanned seven years, and he works to promote student success by
  providing a safe and supportive learning environment; in his
  leisure time, he enjoys reading, writing, attending open mic
  events, and coaching for the PBS quiz competition High Q; and
         WHEREAS, Manuel Delgadillo exemplifies the passion,
  determination, and innovative spirit that are the hallmarks of our
  best teachers, and he has set a standard of excellence to which all
  in his profession may aspire; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Manuel
  Delgadillo on his selection as the 2025-2026 Secondary Teacher of
  the Year in the Ysleta Independent School District and extend to him
  sincere best wishes for continued success with his important work;
  and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for Mr. Delgadillo as an expression of high regard by the
  Texas House of Representatives.
 
  Perez of El Paso

  Speaker of the House

  September 3, 2025, by a non-record vote.
