H.R. No. 93
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, Sara Van Dusen of Killeen is being inducted into the
  U.S. Army Operational Testers' Hall of Fame at Fort Hood on
  August 15, 2025; and
         WHEREAS, Established in 1994, the hall of fame is sponsored
  by the U.S. Army Operational Test Command, and it honors service
  members and civilians who have distinguished themselves in the
  field of operational testing; these outstanding professionals have
  played an instrumental role in evaluating the effectiveness of
  various military systems and equipment, ensuring that soldiers have
  access to the best possible tools in both training and combat
  conditions; and
         WHEREAS, Ms. Van Dusen began her career in operational
  testing in 1984, and over the years, she has been recognized for her
  groundbreaking work in multiple test directorates; she has also
  mentored numerous other professionals in the field and helped to
  shape the Operational Test Command's methods for conducting tests
  and communicating results; she retired from OTC in 2021, after more
  than four and a half decades of exemplary service; and
         WHEREAS, Through her professionalism, technical expertise,
  and commitment to excellence, Sara Van Dusen has significantly
  contributed to the mission of the U.S. Army, and it is a pleasure to
  join in honoring her for her many achievements; now, therefore, be
  it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Sara Van Dusen
  on her induction into the U.S. Army Operational Testers' Hall of
  Fame and extend to her sincere best wishes for the future; and, be
  it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for Ms. Van Dusen as an expression of high regard by the
  Texas House of Representatives.
 
  Buckley
