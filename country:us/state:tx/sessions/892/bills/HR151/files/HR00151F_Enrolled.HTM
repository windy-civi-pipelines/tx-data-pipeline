H.R. No. 151
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, The Rotary Club of Killeen Heights is celebrating 40
  years of service to the community in 2025; and
         WHEREAS, In 1985, members of the Rotary Club of Killeen
  initiated the formation of a second Rotary chapter in their area,
  choosing the name Killeen Heights to represent the members' ties to
  both Killeen and Harker Heights; on July 27, the club formally
  received its charter, with a founding group of 34 business and
  professional leaders; and
         WHEREAS, Over the past four decades, the Rotary Club of
  Killeen Heights has embodied the Rotary motto of "Service Above
  Self" through its efforts to better the lives of Central Texans in
  need; the club strives to advance Rotary International's key
  humanitarian priorities, which include peace and conflict
  resolution, disease prevention, maternal and child health,
  environmental conservation, and economic development, and every
  year, members undertake a well-rounded program of activities that
  make a meaningful impact both at home and abroad; and
         WHEREAS, The Rotary Club of Killeen Heights has long been a
  force for good in its community and beyond, and through their
  generosity and dedication to their organization's ideals, its
  members have set an outstanding example to which all may aspire;
  now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby honor the Rotary Club of
  Killeen Heights on the occasion of its 40th anniversary in 2025 and
  extend to all those affiliated with the club sincere best wishes for
  continued success in their important work; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for the club as an expression of high regard by the Texas
  House of Representatives.
 
  Buckley
