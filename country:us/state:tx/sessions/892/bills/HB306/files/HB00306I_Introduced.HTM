A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 
AN ACT
  relating to the residential and commercial building codes of
  municipalities.
         BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:
         SECTION 1.  Section 214.212, Local Government Code, is
  amended by amending Subsections (a), (b), and (d) and adding
  Subsection (b-1) to read as follows:
         (a)  To protect the public health, safety, and welfare, the
  International Residential Code, as it existed on May 1, 2018
  [2012], is adopted as a municipal residential building code in this
  state.
         (b)  Except as provided by Subsection (b-1), the [The]
  International Residential Code applies to all construction,
  alteration, remodeling, enlargement, and repair of residential
  structures in a municipality.
         (b-1)  To the extent the International Residential Code
  applies to a building or structure on or used in connection with an
  agricultural operation, the International Residential Code, as it
  existed on May 1, 2012, applies to all construction, alteration,
  remodeling, enlargement, and repair of those buildings or
  structures.
         (d)  A municipality may review and consider amendments made
  by the International Code Council to the International Residential
  Code after May 1, 2018 [2012].
         SECTION 2.  Section 214.216, Local Government Code, is
  amended by amending Subsections (a), (b), and (d) and adding
  Subsection (b-1) to read as follows:
         (a)  To protect the public health, safety, and welfare, the
  International Building Code, as it existed on May 1, 2018 [2012], is
  adopted as a municipal commercial building code in this state.
         (b)  Except as provided by Subsection (b-1), the [The]
  International Building Code applies to all commercial buildings in
  a municipality and to any alteration, remodeling, enlargement, or
  repair of those commercial buildings.
         (b-1)  To the extent the International Building Code applies
  to a building or structure on or used in connection with an
  agricultural operation, the International Building Code, as it
  existed on May 1, 2012, applies to all construction, alteration,
  remodeling, enlargement, and repair of those buildings or
  structures.
         (d)  A municipality may review and consider amendments made
  by the International Code Council to the International Building
  Code after May 1, 2018 [2012].
         SECTION 3.  (a)  Sections 214.212 and 214.216, Local
  Government Code, as amended by this Act, apply only to residential
  or commercial construction, remodeling, alteration, enlargement,
  or repair that begins under an agreement made on or after January 1,
  2027, or that begins, in the absence of an agreement, on or after
  that date.  Residential or commercial construction, remodeling,
  alteration, enlargement, or repair that begins under an agreement
  made before January 1, 2027, or that begins, in the absence of an
  agreement, before that date is governed by the law in effect when
  the agreement was made or the activity began, as appropriate, and
  that law is continued in effect for that purpose.
         (b)  Not later than January 1, 2027, municipalities shall
  establish rules and take other necessary actions to implement
  Sections 214.212 and 214.216, Local Government Code, as amended by
  this Act.
         SECTION 4.  This Act takes effect on the 91st day after the
  last day of the legislative session.
