H.R. No. 47
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, All who shared in the life of Eulogio G. Flores of
  San Antonio were deeply saddened by his passing on July 3, 2025, at
  the age of 79; and
         WHEREAS, The son of Daniel O. and Isabel Flores, Eulogio
  Flores was born in Atascosa County on March 11, 1946; and
         WHEREAS, Mr. Flores shared a rewarding marriage with his
  wife, Gloria, that spanned more than six decades; he took great
  pride in their four children, Martin, Dinah, Daniel, and Jesse, and
  with the passing years, he had the pleasure of seeing his family
  grow to include 12 grandchildren and a great-grandchild; and
         WHEREAS, Dedicated to his loved ones, Mr. Flores enjoyed
  ranching with his son, and he was an avid supporter of his
  grandchildren at all of their events; and
         WHEREAS, Those fortunate enough to have known Eulogio Flores
  will remember the way he touched their lives with his warmth and
  generosity of spirit, and he will forever hold a treasured place in
  their hearts; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory
  of Eulogio G. Flores and extend sincere condolences to the members
  of his family: to his wife, Gloria B. Flores; to his children,
  Martin Flores and his wife, Gracie, Dinah Buentello, Daniel Flores
  and his wife, Brenda, and Jesse Flores and his wife, Henriette; to
  his grandchildren, Andrew, Mikey, Emily, Daniel, Sydnie, Seth,
  Malachi, Sabrina, Nolan, Eden, Gideon, and Lexi; to his
  great-granddaughter, Gianina; and to his many other relatives and
  friends; and, be it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for his family and that when the Texas House of
  Representatives adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Eulogio
  Flores.
 
  Lujan
 
  ______________________________
  Speaker of the House

  vote of the House on August 27, 2025.
