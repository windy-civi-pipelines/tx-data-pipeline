H.R. No. 152
 
 
 
R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, The 7th annual Bell County Area Go Texan BBQ
  Cook-off is taking place in Belton on October 18, 2025; and
         WHEREAS, Sanctioned by Champions Barbecue Alliance as one of
  the organization's state championship cook-offs, this
  much-anticipated event attracts talented chefs from across Texas
  and beyond; these highly skilled pitmasters put their culinary
  abilities to the test as they vie to produce the tastiest entries in
  several different categories, including chicken, ribs, and
  brisket; and
         WHEREAS, The grand champion of the contest will receive an
  invitation to the American Royal World Series of Barbecue as well as
  a chance to take part in the Jack Daniel's World Championship
  Invitational Barbecue competition; and
         WHEREAS, The cook-off serves as a fundraiser for Bell County
  Area Go Texan, whose primary mission is to raise scholarship funds
  for area graduating seniors; and
         WHEREAS, Texans are noted for their love of barbecue, and the
  Bell County Area Go Texan BBQ Cook-off is sure to offer everyone in
  attendance the chance to enjoy good food and good times while
  supporting a worthwhile cause; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commemorate the 7th annual
  Bell County Area Go Texan BBQ Cook-off and extend to the organizers,
  contestants, and attendees sincere best wishes for a fun and
  successful event.
 
  Buckley
