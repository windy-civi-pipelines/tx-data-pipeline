Title: 89(2) HR 98 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 98 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 98 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, McKinley Wagley was crowned the 2025 Miss Rodeo North Texas Fair Princess at the 97th annual North Texas Fair & Rodeo in Denton; and WHEREAS, McKinley claimed the title after sweeping the competition by placing first in the horsemanship, appearance, personality, Q&A, and photogenic categories; and WHEREAS, The daughter of Katherine and Jason Wagley, McKinley is a fifth-grade student at Monaco Elementary School in Aubrey, and she first became interested in rodeo in 2020 after attending a summer horse camp; her many interests include roping and competitive dancing, and she hopes to one day compete for the titles of Miss Rodeo Texas Princess and Miss Rodeo America; and WHEREAS, McKinley Wagley is a source of great pride to her family and her community, and she is indeed deserving of recognition for this notable achievement; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate McKinley Wagley on her selection as the 2025 Miss Rodeo North Texas Fair Princess and extend to her sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Ms. Wagley as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. Patterson ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 98 was adopted by the House on September 2, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 98 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 98 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, McKinley Wagley was crowned the 2025 Miss Rodeo North Texas Fair Princess at the 97th annual North Texas Fair & Rodeo in Denton; and WHEREAS, McKinley claimed the title after sweeping the competition by placing first in the horsemanship, appearance, personality, Q&A, and photogenic categories; and WHEREAS, The daughter of Katherine and Jason Wagley, McKinley is a fifth-grade student at Monaco Elementary School in Aubrey, and she first became interested in rodeo in 2020 after attending a summer horse camp; her many interests include roping and competitive dancing, and she hopes to one day compete for the titles of Miss Rodeo Texas Princess and Miss Rodeo America; and WHEREAS, McKinley Wagley is a source of great pride to her family and her community, and she is indeed deserving of recognition for this notable achievement; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate McKinley Wagley on her selection as the 2025 Miss Rodeo North Texas Fair Princess and extend to her sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Ms. Wagley as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives.
