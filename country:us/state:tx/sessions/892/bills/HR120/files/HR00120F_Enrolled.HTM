Title: 89(2) HR 120 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 120 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 120 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Words cannot adequately express the sorrow felt at the passing of Mary Kathryn Jacobe of Houston, who was tragically lost in the July 4, 2025, flooding in Kerr County at the age of eight; and WHEREAS, A fifth-generation Houstonian, Mary Kate was born to Samantha and Christopher Jacobe on October 27, 2016; she was best friends with her older brother, Russel, and she cherished her puppies, Rex and Pickles; the baby of the family, she was doted on by her grandparents, Russel and Brenda Jacobe and Richard and Kathryn Jordan, as well as by her aunts, uncles, and cousins; and WHEREAS, Mary Kate attended St. Martin's Episcopal Preschool and subsequently enrolled at The Fay School from kindergarten through second grade; possessing a confidence and wisdom beyond her years, she was a friend to all, and her loyalty and kindness knew no bounds; feisty and charming by equal measures, she possessed a determination that heralded a bright future, and her ever-evolving list of potential occupations included those of doctor, singer, actress, and artist; and WHEREAS, Although it is immeasurably painful to lose someone so young and full of promise, those left behind may find comfort in knowing that their lives have been forever enriched by the joyful times that they shared with this beloved child; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of Mary Kathryn Jacobe and extend heartfelt condolences to the members of her family: to her parents, Samantha Jordan Jacobe and Christopher Craig Jacobe; to her brother, Russel Lee Jacobe; to her grandparents, Brenda Craig Jacobe and Richard Jordan; to her aunts and uncles, Dr. Russel Lee Jacobe III and his wife, Heidi, Michael Copeman Jacobe and his wife, Mindy, Janice Jacobe Davis and her husband, Clark, Jon Paul Jordan and his wife, Tamara, and Justin Jordan and his wife, Maggie; to her cousins, Peyton, Reid, Sydney, and Jack Jacobe, Jacobe, Clark, and Cornelia Davis, and J.P., Kathryn, Josh, Lizzy, and Jaden Jordan; and to her other relatives and many friends; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for her family and that when the Texas House of Representatives adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Mary Kate Jacobe. Wu ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 120 was unanimously adopted by a rising vote of the House on September 2, 2025. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 120 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 120 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Words cannot adequately express the sorrow felt at the passing of Mary Kathryn Jacobe of Houston, who was tragically lost in the July 4, 2025, flooding in Kerr County at the age of eight;
