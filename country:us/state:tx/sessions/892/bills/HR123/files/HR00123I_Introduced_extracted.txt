89(2) HR 123 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S21192 CJM-D By: Button H.R. No. 123 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Scott LeMay retired as mayor of Garland on June 17, 2025; and WHEREAS, A resident of Garland since 2001, Mr. LeMay has long taken an active role in his community, serving on the board of the Camelot Neighborhood Association and on the Garland Charter Review Committee and Plan Commission; he subsequently made a successful bid to become a Garland City Council member, a position he held for three consecutive terms; following his election as mayor on May 4, 2019, he demonstrated outstanding leadership in carrying out the duties of his office; and WHEREAS, Outside of his civic involvement, Mr. LeMay has enjoyed a successful career designing layouts for Container Stores across the country; in all his endeavors, he has benefited from the love and support of a fine family that includes his wife, Tiffany, and their three children, Caitlyn, Morgan, and Reagan; and WHEREAS, The dedication, expertise, and commitment to excellence demonstrated by Scott LeMay have greatly benefited the citizens of Garland, and he may indeed reflect with pride on a job well done; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby commend Scott LeMay for his service as mayor of Garland and extend to him sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Mr. LeMay as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives.
