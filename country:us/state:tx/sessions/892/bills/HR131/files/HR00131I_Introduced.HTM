R E S O L U T I O N
         WHEREAS, The 100th birthday of an esteemed Texan is truly
  cause for celebration, and Alicia Canales Gonzalez of Hebbronville
  marked this momentous milestone in her life on March 26, 2025; and
         WHEREAS, Born in Hebbronville in 1925, the former Alicia
  Canales is the daughter of Santos Canales and Amalia Ramos Canales,
  and she grew up as the 9th of 11 children; in 1949, she married Jose
  "Joe" G. Gonzalez, and they were blessed with two daughters, Irma
  Alicia and Linda Jo; and
         WHEREAS, Mrs. Gonzalez has earned the admiration of her
  fellow citizens through her contributions to the community;
  following her husband's death in 1982, she was appointed to
  complete his fifth term in office as Jim Hogg County treasurer and
  served for more than six months; a devout Catholic, she is a
  lifelong member of Our Lady of Guadalupe Catholic Parish, and she
  was active with the Mother's Club, which assisted with children's
  activities at the Little Flower School; she has also served in the
  Sociedad Guadalupanas, Secular Franciscan Order, and Sociedad
  Josefa Ortiz de Domínguez; in addition, she enjoyed participating
  in the folklórico dance group at the Jim Hogg County Nutrition
  Center; and
         WHEREAS, Today, Mrs. Gonzalez is the treasured matriarch of a
  fine family that has grown to include four grandchildren,
  Fortunato, Gerardo, JoAnn, and Susana, as well as six
  great-grandchildren, Emilio, Lucas, Joe, Shayla, Trisha, and
  JoDee, and a great-great-grandson, Kenneth; witty and charming, she
  has always been a source of joy for friends and relatives alike; and
         WHEREAS, Blessed with the affection of a host of loved ones
  and sustained by her strong faith, Alicia Gonzalez has gracefully
  reached the century mark and continues to enrich the lives of all
  who are fortunate enough to know her; now, therefore, be it
         RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas
  Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Alicia
  Canales Gonzalez on the joyous occasion of her 100th birthday and
  extend to her sincere best wishes for continued happiness; and, be
  it further
         RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be
  prepared for Mrs. Gonzalez as an expression of high regard by the
  Texas House of Representatives.
