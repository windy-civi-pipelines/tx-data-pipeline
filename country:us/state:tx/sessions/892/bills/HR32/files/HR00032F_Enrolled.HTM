Title: 89(2) HR 32 - Enrolled version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Enrolled Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 32 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 32 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Horace Hodge is retiring from the United States Department of Agriculture in 2025, drawing to a close an exemplary tenure with the agency spanning more than 48 years; and WHEREAS, Mr. Hodge developed an early interest in agriculture and was active in Future Farmers of America throughout his youth and during his time as a student at Prairie View A&M University; after earning his bachelor's degree in agriculture education in 1978, he began his career with the USDA Soil Conservation Service; and WHEREAS, In 1989, Mr. Hodge returned to the Prairie View A&M campus when he joined the USDA Office of Partnerships and Public Engagement as a liaison in the 1890 Program, an initiative that seeks to increase the number of students studying agriculture and related disciplines at historically Black colleges and universities; among his many responsibilities, he has served as an advisor for agriculture students, encouraging them to participate in special learning opportunities in their fields of study; in recognition of his efforts, he received a 2024 Dean's Award in Excellence from the PVAMU College of Agriculture, Food, and Natural Resources; and WHEREAS, Through his leadership, dedication, and commitment to assisting students, Horace Hodge has advanced the work of the USDA and greatly benefited the PVAMU community, and he may indeed reflect with pride on his many achievements as he embarks on the next exciting chapter of his life; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby congratulate Horace Hodge on his retirement from the U.S. Department of Agriculture and extend to him sincere best wishes for the future; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for Mr. Hodge as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives. Bell of Montgomery ______________________________ Speaker of the House I certify that H.R. No. 32 was adopted by the House on August 27, 2025, by a non-record vote. ______________________________ Chief Clerk of the House ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 32 - Enrolled version - Bill Text H.R. No. 32 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Horace Hodge is retiring from the United States Department of Agriculture in 2025, drawing to a close an exemplary tenure with the agency spanning more than 48 years;
