Title: 89(2) HR 17 - Introduced version - Bill Text Official Title: Number of Sections: 1 Source: versions - Introduced Media Type: text/html ================================================================================ Section 1: 89(2) HR 17 - Introduced version - Bill Text By: Money H.R. No. 17 R E S O L U T I O N BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, That Rule 5, Section 3, of the permanent rules of the House of Representatives of the 89th Legislature is amended by amending Subsection (d) and adding Subsections (d-1) and (d-2) to read as follows: (d)  If a member is absent without leave for the purpose of impeding the action of the house, the member is subject to one or more of the following: (1)  fines as provided by this section; (2)  payment of costs incurred by the sergeant-at-arms; (3)  reprimand; (4)  censure; [or] (5)  removal of seniority acquired by cumulative years of service in the house, in the manner prescribed by Subsection (d-1) of this section; (6)  forfeiture of office space, parking space, and desk assignments made to the member by the Committee on House Administration, in the manner prescribed by Subsection (d-2) of this section; or (7)  expulsion in the manner prescribed by Section 11, Article III, Texas Constitution. (d-1)  A member absent without leave for the purpose of impeding the action of the house is considered to have two years of seniority less than the member would otherwise have for each day that a quorum is not present while the member is absent. (d-2)  The office space, parking space, and desk on the floor of the house belonging to a member who is absent without leave for the purpose of impeding the action of the house on three or more days in a single legislative session that a quorum is not present are forfeited. ================================================================================ Raw Text: 89(2) HR 17 - Introduced version - Bill Text By: Money H.R. No. 17 R E S O L U T I O N BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, That Rule 5, Section 3, of the permanent rules of the House of Representatives of the 89th Legislature is amended by amending Subsection (d) and adding Subsections (d-1) and (d-2) to read as follows: (d)  If a member is absent without leave for the purpose of impeding the action of the house, the member is subject to one or more of the following: (1)  fines as provided by this section; (2)  payment of costs incurred by the sergeant-at-arms; (3)  reprimand; (4)  censure; [or] (5)  removal of seniority acquired by cumulative years of service in the house, in the manner prescribed by Subsection (d-1) of this section; (6)  forfeiture of office space, parking space, and desk assignments made to the member by the Committee on House Administration, in the manner prescribed by Subsection (d-2) of this section; or (7)  expulsion in the manner prescribed by Section 11, Article III, Texas Constitution.
