A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 
AN ACT
  relating to the ballot proposition for a school district bond
  election.
         BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:
         SECTION 1.  Section 45.003(b-1), Education Code, is amended
  to read as follows:
         (b-1)  The ballot proposition under Subsection (b) must
  include:
               (1)  the following statement: "THIS IS A PROPERTY TAX
  INCREASE."; and
               (2)  the percentage of students in the district who
  were required to take the mathematics and reading assessment
  instruments administered under Section 39.023(a) during the most
  recent school year and performed satisfactorily on those assessment
  instruments.
         SECTION 2.  This Act takes effect on the 91st day after the
  last day of the legislative session.
