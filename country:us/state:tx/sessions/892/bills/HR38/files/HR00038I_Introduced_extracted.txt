89(2) HR 38 - Introduced version - Bill Text 89S20206 EME-D By: Meyer H.R. No. 38 R E S O L U T I O N WHEREAS, Words cannot adequately express the sorrow felt by the family and friends of Hadley Rebecca Hanna of University Park, who passed away on July 4, 2025, at the age of 8; and WHEREAS, Born on February 13, 2017, to William and Carrie Hanna, Hadley Hanna was blessed with the companionship of her two sisters, Harper and Hunter; she also benefited from the love and support of her grandparents and the other members of her extended family; and WHEREAS, A student at University Park Elementary School, this independent and spirited young girl was known on campus for her effervescent smile, sneak attack hugs, cheer teams formed at recess, and a penchant for teaching her friends how to tie their shoes; moreover, she was passionate about dancing, tumbling at Mustang Cheer, playing basketball, and participating in theater; possessed of a sweet and often silly personality, she cultivated happiness for others through her kindness, creativity, and ability to make people laugh; and WHEREAS, Hadley found a special home at Camp Mystic, a Christian summer camp near Kerrville, where she became an avid angler and was a proud member of the Tonk tribe; above all, she treasured being with her family and friends, and her untimely passing has inspired an outpouring of prayers and sympathy from the community at large; Hadley was a cherished congregant of Highland Park United Methodist Church; and WHEREAS, Although her journey on this earth ended far too soon, Hadley Hanna was a bright light and a source of joy in the lives of all who knew her, and she will forever hold a special place in their hearts; now, therefore, be it RESOLVED, That the House of Representatives of the 89th Texas Legislature, 2nd Called Session, hereby pay tribute to the memory of Hadley Rebecca Hanna and extend profound condolences to the members of her family: to her parents, William Douglas Hanna and Carrie Crossman Hanna; to her sisters, Harper and Hunter Hanna; to her grandparents, Pam and Stanley Crossman, Jeanie and Duane Tinkler, and Bill and Donna Hanna; to her aunts, uncles, and cousins; and to her many other relatives and friends; and, be it further RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for her family and that when the Texas House of Representatives adjourns this day, it do so in memory of Hadley Hanna.
